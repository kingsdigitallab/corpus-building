Text of CIL

English translation

To the spirits of the Underworld. Valerius Victorinus, the heir, made this for the well-deserving Aurelius Rusticus, who served for eight years in the century aboard the trireme Zenon, (and for) Marcus Germanicianus, who served for ten years.
