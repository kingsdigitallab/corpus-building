English translation

Stenis Kalinis (son) of Stattis (and) Maras Pompiis (son) of Niumdis, meddices, and the Mamertine people built (this). Sacred to Apollo.

[Latinised] Stenius Calinius (son) of Stattius (and) Maras Pompius (son) of Niumidius, meddices, and the Mamertine people built (this). Sacred to Apollo.

commentary
