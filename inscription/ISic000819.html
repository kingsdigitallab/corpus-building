commentary

In 1962, Manganaro suggested that the decoration on the side represented the tools of an agrimensor, in support of an attempt to interpret lines 7-8 as a doric form of geometres, viz gametras. As Manganaro 2001 observed, in fact this was an erroneous reading, induced by the very irregular layout of the text.
