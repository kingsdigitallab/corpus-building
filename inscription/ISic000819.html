Apparatus criticus

Text based upon photograph and rapid autopsy; Manganaro 1962 resolved the last two lines as γαμέτρας

Italian translation

Chariton, lodevole e irreprensibile, visse sessant'anni, 3 mesi, 1 giorno

English translation

Chariton, commendable and irreproachable, lived sixty years, 3 months, 1 day

commentary

In 1962, Manganaro suggested that the decoration on the side represented the tools of an agrimensor, in support of an attempt to interpret lines 7-8 as a doric form of geometres, viz gametras. As Manganaro 2001 observed, in fact this was an erroneous reading, induced by the very irregular layout of the text.
