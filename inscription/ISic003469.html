English translation

To Ceres Iulia Augusta, (wife of the) deified Augustus, mother of Tiberius Caesar Augustus, Lutatia, daughter of Gaius, sacerdos of Augusta the wife of the perpeptual Imperator, wife of Marcus Livius Optatus, son of Marcus, of tribe Quirina, flamen of the Gaulitani of Iulia Augusta wife of the eternal Imperator, consecrated [this] with [her husband?] and children, from her own money
