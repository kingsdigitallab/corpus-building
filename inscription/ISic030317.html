Description
Four fragments of a lead lamina, inscribed on one side, of which fragments 2-3 join, and can be related to fr. 1; fr. 4 remains unconnected but part of the whole. Frag. 1, H 3.5 cm, W 1 cm; frag. 2, H 2.6 cm, W 1.7 cm; frag. 3 , H 2.4 cm, W 1.5 cm; frag. 4, H 2.3 cm, W 2 cm.
