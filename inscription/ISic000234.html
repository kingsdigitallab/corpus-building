Apparatus criticus

Text after autopsy; annis (or annos) is presumably abbreviated, but the precise form would be speculation.

English translation

To the shades of the underworld. Marcus Villius [---] lived for [---] years

commentary

There are no other attestations of this gens in Sicily. Bivona (1994) notes that there was a P. Villius Tappulus who was praetor in Sicily in 203 BCE, but correctly emphasises that this is 'too remote a past to be able to reasonably find a connection between the two'.
