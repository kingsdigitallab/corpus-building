Θεαρίδας Ποδ[---]

[τρίτ?]ας´ ἀπὸ νε̄́(τας)

Apparatus criticus

Text after Cordano 1992

English translation

Italian translation

commentary

This document is one of a large deposit (c. 150) of lead tesserae found in a storeroom of Athena's sanctuary at the archaeological site of Kamarina. On face a there are traces of a former use.
