Description
A stele in the form of a 'horned' altar, in local limestone. The lefthand horn is slightly damaged. The upper part of the face is deocrated with a series of five incised horizontal lines to imitate mouldings. The space between the top two of these is decorated by a broad incised zig-zig. Most of the face is left plain and roughly finished/smoothed, to be occupied by the inscription. There is a projection at the base, partly damaged.
