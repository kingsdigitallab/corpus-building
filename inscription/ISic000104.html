[---]Cn(aei) ((·)) f(ilius) ((·)) IIir ((·)) v ((·)) quinq(uennalis) ((·)) +[---]

Apparatus criticus

Text from autopsy; The vertical stroke of a further letter is preserved on the break on the right hand edge, but given the lack of evidence from serifs, the possibilities are multiple; the last interpunct and this trace of a further letter both omitted by previous editors.

English translation

...son of Gnaeus, duumvir quinquennalis...

commentary

The absence of a cognomen should be noted. The slight traces of curvature along the edges suggests a circular structure?
