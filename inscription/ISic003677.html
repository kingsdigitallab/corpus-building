commentary

The text is reported by Carettoni amongst material recovered from the multiple trenches along the so-called 'muro a bugnato', on the east side of the area of the agora. It has not been observed since, but should be in store on site. Carettoni suggests, presumably on the basis of the material ('marmo greco') that it may belong to the same text is ISic003675, although the thickness is slightly different between the two (but not enough to rule this out).
