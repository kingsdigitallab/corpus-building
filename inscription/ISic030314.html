commentary

This document is part of a group of four tesserae (ISic030311-ISic030314) found during a survey in 1990 (5th of September) in the area north north of the temple of Athena, where a large deposit (c. 150) of lead tesserae was previously found. Besides the tesserae in the same area four bronze coins, a bronze ring, a sling bullet (not inscribed) and metal scraps were found.
