English translation

To Publius Cestius Catullus, approximately(?) 27 years of age, and his father Publius Cestius Catullus.

commentary

There is no immediately clear answer as to what the sign "â†ƒ", a reverse C, symbolises here; cf. ISic000163 and ISic000174 as well as CIL 6.15139 (Rome) for analogous use, as pointed out by Bivona (1994), who suggests that it stands for 'circiter'. The gens Cestius is well attested in Termini, often with the cognomen Catullus.
