Apparatus criticus

Text based on autopsy

translation

Eukarpia, a good woman, farewell, she lived [--] years

commentary

The layout of the text suggests ἔζησεν in full, but other Greek funerary texts from Centuripe suggest that ἔζησες might be possible.
