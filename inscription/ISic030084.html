Provenance found
Found in the Necropolis of S. Placido (excavations of P. Orsi, 1914), in burial no. 48, a tomb 'a cappuccina' in 'recinto F', which was covered by a pile of stones; the lamina was found inside the burial, directly beneath the ceramic tube provided for libations.
