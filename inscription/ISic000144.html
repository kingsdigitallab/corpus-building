Apparatus criticus

Text from autopsy; Gualtherus, Romano, Mommsen: [D] M; the lower right part of the D is visible on the break.

English translation

To the Shades of the Underworld. Publius Cestius [---]us

commentary

The gens Cestius is well attested at Termini Imerese.
