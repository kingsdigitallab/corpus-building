Description
A large rectangula block of a coarse crystalline grey stone with a brown patina to the surface. Intact top, left and probably upper right and behind. May be intact lower left, and the lower front edge is worn, at the least. Lower right corner lost. Sides and top roughly finished, front face smooth.
