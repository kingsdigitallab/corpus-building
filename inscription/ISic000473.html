Description
A large slab of white marble, intact on all sides (a break on the lower right corner/right side has been repaired). Two rectangular clamp holes are visible on the top, and a further two on the base. The front is finished, but not polished; the sides are rough finished; the rear is smooth.

Lettering

Tall, slightly curved letters, mostly with minimal serifs. P is open; R is closed with long tail; E has short horizontals of equal length, slightly upward curving; M is four equal slanting strokes. In line 5 the O and C of 'procos' are intelinked, and the following O is small and mid-line. A large diagonal upward stroke is visible above the first letter of each of the civic ethnics; and a horizontal above the Q at the end of line 6. The letters all show traces of dark paint (modern?).
