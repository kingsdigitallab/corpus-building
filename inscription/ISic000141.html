English translation

Caninia Epitychia lived 18 years, 13 days.

Physical description

Support

Description
Six joining fragments of a rectangular marble plaque, which is complete except for two small gaps in the upper edge. The face is reddish in colour, although the stone is not; the rear is smooth.
