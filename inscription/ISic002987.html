commentary

The text belongs to the earlier sequence of account blocks, using the local month names. Columns 1 and 2 contain the closing months of the year, while columns 3 and 4 have the opening five months of the following year, as indicated by the fragmentary reference to the eponym of the year at the start of column 3.
