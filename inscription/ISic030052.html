Apparatus criticus

Text based on Manganaro in Parola del Passato 1989, corrected from photo in Cordano 1984; Perhaps [ἐσχη]κότα, an accusative participle referring to the seller's possession of the property in question?
5: κα[ὶ], M., but the iota is clearly visible in the photo.
6: τῶ<ι>, M., but the iota is clearly visible in the photo.
7: [Ε]ὐάμερ[ος?], M., but the epsilon is clearly visible in the photo.

English translation

...Kotas(?)...and the water...in the land...and the...of Euameros...talents. Guarantors:...

commentary

Tiny fragment, but the unambiguous reference to guarantors (Ἄμποχοι) suggests this is an independent contract and not to be joined with another existing text. It is written in the same hand as ISic 030051.
