English translation

Sacred to the spirits of the dead. For Lucius Cornelius Campestrus, cooper, his wife Claudia Paezusa (dedicated this). He lived 25(?)...

Italian translation

Sacro agli spiriti dei defunti. Per Lucius Cornelius Campestrus, cuparius, la moglie Claudia Paezusa (pose questo). Visse 25(?)...
