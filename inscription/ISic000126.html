English translation

Publius Alfesius Salvus, son of Publius, farewell! He lived nineteen years.

Lettering
Carving of letters is rough, getting rougher and more inconsistent. Text is angular and haphazard, more pronounced in line 4. Letter heights and depths are inconsistent. Use of serifs and the apex on the 'A' in line 4 is particularly pronounced. Triangular interpuncts of different shapes: lines 1-2, interpuncts are outline triangles, line 4, interpuncts are hollowed out.
