commentary

In contrast to the other texts reported in the 18th-century Trapani manuscript tradition, this text is quite possibly not a forgery; it is however most likely to be post-antique, as suggested by Korhonen (di epoca postmedievale). It was omitted by Kaibel, presumably on these grounds.
