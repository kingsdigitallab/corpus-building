Text: CIL

English translation

Face a: Dedicated on the 7th day before the Ides of September. To Imperator Antoninus Augustus, consul for the fourth time, and Decimus Caelius Balbinus, consul for the second time, by the means of Marcus Antius Gratillianus, quaestor pro praetore of the province of Sicily, vir clarissimus.

Face b: To Imperator Caesar Marcus Aurelius Antoninus Pius Felix Invictus Augustus, with tribunician power for the 17th time, consular power for the 4th, proconsul and father of the fatherland. The colony of Helvia Augusta Lilybitanorum devotes this to his divine power and majesty.
