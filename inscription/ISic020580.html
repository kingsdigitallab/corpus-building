commentary

An inscription painted on different parts of the same vessel, whose repetitiveness, together with its content, betrays its ludic nature. The game could perhaps refer to a recreational situation in a symposial context in which Aka could be the name of the courtesan (thus Dubois) or simply, without over-interpreting the inscription, the name of the beloved of Geloios, who perhaps due to his young age or lack of means would have asked Eurimachos to pay for the hydria.
