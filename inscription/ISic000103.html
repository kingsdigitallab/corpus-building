C(aio) ((路)) Virio ((路)) C(ai) ((路)) f(ilio) ((路)) Cla(udia)
Maximo ((路)) haruspic(i)
Clodiae ((路)) M(arci) ((路)) l(ibertae) ((路)) Donatae ((路)) m(erentibus?)

Apparatus criticus

Text from autopsy; Mommsen: 'm(erentibus) potius quam m(aritae)'

English translation

To Gaius Virius Maximus, son of Gaius, of the Claudia tribe, haruspex, (and) to Clodia Donata, freedwoman of Marcus, well-deserving.
