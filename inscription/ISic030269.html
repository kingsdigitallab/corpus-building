commentary

This document is one of a large deposit (c. 150) of lead tesserae found in a storeroom of Athena's sanctuary at the archaeological site of Kamarina. The occurrence of the same name on both faces a and b would suggest, according to the editor, a double use of the lamina by the same person. However, it is worth noting that the name is integrated on both faces.
