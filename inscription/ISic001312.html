commentary

The formula is a distinctively Latin one. Ferrua suggests the text is of Christian date (on the basis that Amico attributed it to a christian cemetery; but the necropolis outside Porta Stesicora covers a much wider period than this), but Korhonen reasonably suggests that it need not be so.
