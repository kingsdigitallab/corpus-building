Apparatus criticus

Comparison to Mommsen and Gualtherus. Mommsen excludes sacrum which exists in Gaultherus, Mommsen restores Logismus for Locismus, Mommsen suggests Erotinoi for Protioni

English translation

Sacred to the shades of the underworld. Lucius Arrius Lo(g)ismus built this for his daughter [... who lived for] 8 months, 18 days and for Arrius Protio, friend, who lived for 25 years
