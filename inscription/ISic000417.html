Description
Large fragment of a cyrstalline white marble, intact along the upper edge, broken left and below, and upper right corner. Note that the right edge is a very clean edge, suggesting that this is the end of the slab in question, with a further slab adjoining. Finished on the reverse; the upper rear edge is chipped.

commentary
