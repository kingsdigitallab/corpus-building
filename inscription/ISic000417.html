Text from autopsy; It is obviously impossible to know the inflected form of Caesar
2: The letter is either I, or N, or L; given the position at the end of the slab, I or N seems more likely than L

translation

commentary

It would be tempting to associate the fragment, which seems likely to be part of a monumental imperial building inscription, with the early Imperial creation of a porticoed square in front of the great altar.
