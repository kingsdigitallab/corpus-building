Inscription

Layout
Remains of three lines of large Greek letters with regular left margin. The text is not perpendicular to the face of the block as it survives, either implying poor layout or else the block has been recut.
Text condition
incomplete
Lettering
Large wide and square letters, regularly set out, without notable serifs. Alpha has broken bar; epsilon has shorter middle bar; kappa has short arms; omicron is slightly smaller than other letters and above the line; pi has equal length vertical strokes and slight overlap of the horizontal; rho has a small closed loop
