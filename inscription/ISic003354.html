English translation

To Caius Vallius Postumus [son ?], of tribe Quirina, patron of the municipium, perpetual flamen of Divus Hadrian, raised to the rank of quadringenarius amongst the five decuria of judges by the divine Antoninus Augustus Pius, having most honourably held all the honours of a citizen of his own town, as well as having at his own expense served as legate both to the deified Hadrian and the Senate regarding the repayment of taxes, the plebs Gaulita [set this up] by public subscription on account of his many merits and by decree of the decuriones
