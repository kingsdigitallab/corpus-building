Description
Two fragments of a marble plaque, suggested by Manganaro to be part of the same text due to physical similarities. Fragment (a) shows a vacat and preserves a moulding and original edge below, suggesting the final line of a text; fragment (b) is broken on all sides. Fragment (b) is W 17 cm x H 9 x D 4 (Pucci).
