Text of IG 14.0444 7: ε Ferrua 1982 and 1989

translation

commentary

Ferrua notes that the date must be corrected, if it is to align with the consulship year of 409. Note the day recorded as day of the moon; and note the combined use of Greek and Latin abbreviations for Augustus, the latter in Greek letters.
