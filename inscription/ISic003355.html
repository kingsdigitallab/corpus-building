Lettering

Lunate omega, sigma and epsilon; alpha, lamda and delta have extended upper strokes; mu appears in two forms on line 2, typical later form with tall upper strokes and at line end a small rounded form mid-line; xsi on line 4 is formed of two horizontals with a detached curved stroke between. The repetition of the deceased's name on line 3 is marked off by a large comma-shaped interpunct.
