English translation

When Archagathos was in office, on the sixteenth (day) of Geloios. --on-- child of ----, fifth (phratry)-first (tribe) bought from Athanios son of Basias the enclosed field in --tuai, two schoinoi in width, and in length from the "Konchokranaios" stream up to the "crooked reeds," for twenty-one talents. Guarantors: Theudosios son of Theudoros, sixth (phratry), Theotimos son of Lamiskos, Ameinon son of Damatrios, Numphodoros son of Damon, Theodoros son of Aristogenes, Artamidoros son of Theodosios, Muskon son of Andron, Prodoxos son of Sosias, Ainesis son of Thallos, Orthon son of --llos.

commentary

One outstanding question concerns the inconsistent application of tribe and phratry designations. Why is the buyer identified by both tribe and phratry, while the first guarantor is identified only by phratry, and the rest of the guarantors are not identified by either tribe or phratry?
