| [--- Αἰ]σχρίωνα καὶ Νυ | μφόδωρο[ν] | Βομβυλίνου

Apparatus criticus

Text of Manganaro 1999, based upon antiquarian record itself reported by Orsi

Italian translation

Escriona e Ninfodoro, figli di Bombulino

English translation

Aeschrion and Nymphodoros, sons of Bombylinos

commentary

Orsi instead proposed restoring in a different order and with different inflections, as Βομβυλίνος, Χριῶνα καὶ Νυμφόδωρος
