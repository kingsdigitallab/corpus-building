English translation

To [---]a Procula, daughter of Lucius... [(name)] Plaetorius, son of Spurius, of the Claudian tribe, quaestor, aedile, duumvir, ... [(name) --]ius Galeo, son of Quintus, of the Quirina tribe, to whom the decurial order decreed aedilician honours, [---]a Longina, daughter of Lucius. [(name)] Proculus, of the Claudian tribe, quaestor four times, prefect pro duumvir, duumvir quinqennalis, military tribune of the Legio VI Ferrata.
