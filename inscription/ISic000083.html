Physical description

Support

Description
A slab of Luna marble, partly corroded in the centre. Slighty convex in form (thinner at the edges). Intact on all sides. The rear is unfinished.
Object type
plaque
Material
marble

Inscription

Layout
Thirteen lines of Latin text, filling the available space.
Text condition
No data
Technique
chiselled
Pigment
No data
Lettering

Deeply engraved letters, somewhat irregular in height. A is consistently rendered without cross-bar. There is some fluidity between E, L, I and T, either one for the other, or simply rendered by a vertical stroke. Occasional use of ligatures. No interpunctuation.

Letter heights
Lines 1-13: 15-20mm

Not measured: mm

Provenance

Place of origin
Catina
Provenance found
Found in July 1730, exact provenance not clear, but likely in the area of the necropolis immediately north of Piazza Stesicoro, either in the vicinity of the basilica on via Dott. Consoli, or else along via Sant'Euplio
