commentary

The first name occurs in ISic020463 in the dative case (or nominative plural if it is to be understood as the name of a genos). The second noun occurs, as Arena notes, also in the feminine form in Athens, Illyria, Delos and Kos. See also IG II(2) 9319, from Megara (IV c. BC); I.Epidamnos 316, from Epidamnos-Dyrrhachion (III-II c. BC); I.Bouthrotos 99, from Epeiros (II c. BC).
