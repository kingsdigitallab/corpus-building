English translation

My lord Euagrios freely gave this tomb to me, Theodoulos, and here lie Kostantis and Kekilia.

Italian translation

Il mio signore Euagrios ha donato questa tomba a me, Theodoulos, e qui giacciono Kostantis e Kaikilia.
