English translation

When ------- was in office, on the sixth of Heraios, Dinarchos son of Kleandros was president of the supervisors of contracts. Sosistratos son of Theon, last (phratry)-first (tribe), bought the house and the whole business of Dion and the doorways, the walls shared with Philoxenos and Thrasyllos, in the district of Pergaos and Phersophasa, from Dion son of Herakleidas, fourth (phratry)-first (tribe), for forty talents. Guarantors: Ariston son of Emmenidas, last (phratry)-first (tribe), Philippos son of Pausanias, last (phratry)-first (tribe), Artamidoros son of Herakleidas, fourth (phratry)-first (tribe), Pausanias son of Sosikrates, last (phratry)-first (tribe), Herakleios son of Nikon, third (phrary)-first (tribe), Sannon son of Zopuros, fourth (phratry)-first (tribe), Simos son of Geloios, last (phratry)-first (tribe), Muon son of Euthumenes, sixth (phratry)-first (tribe), Theudorods son of Damon, sixth (phratry)-first (tribe), Gelon son of Kallistratos, sixth (phratry)-first (tribe).
