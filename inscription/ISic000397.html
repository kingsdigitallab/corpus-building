((cross_greek)) Hic requiescit Iohannis filius Tar-
ric qui vixit ((·)) an(nos) ((·)) tris men(ses) duo dies XVI
dep(ositus) in pace XVII Kal(endas) Ianuarias ind(ictione) XIIII

Apparatus criticus

Text after Manganaro, controlled against photograph

English translation

Here lies Tarric son of Iohannis, who lived for 3 years, 2 months, 16 days. Laid to rest in peace, the 17th day before the Kalends of January, in the 14th indiction.
