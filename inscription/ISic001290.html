Apparatus criticus

Korhonen 2004

English translation

Friends seeking me, here I lie, a child who died early, citizen of great Ephesus. The name of my father is Achilleus and of my mother, Stratonike. Fate gave me to die in this place. Having lived for 16 years, I now lie here, having left the light of life. My father, Publius Aeilius Achileus set this up in my memory.
