Κλωδία Σε-
βῆρα ἔζησ-
σεν ἔτη ((·)) ΙΙ͡Π´
((ivy-leaf)) ( vac. 6) ((ivy-leaf)) ((ivy-leaf))

Apparatus criticus

Text after Bitto

English translation

Klodia Sebera, lived 7 years

commentary

The numeral is here interpreted (following Bitto) as acrophonic and ascending, i.e. two verticals, the second in ligature with a Π for πέντε. The hederae in the final line include a third hedera of different from.
