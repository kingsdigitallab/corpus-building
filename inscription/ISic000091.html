Description
Large white marble statue base. Mostly intact in the lower part and on the right, but damaged top and to the upper left. Most of the front face is perserved, but parts of the surface eroded. A moulding around the base, on the front, right and left sides, but not to the rear. The rear, right and left sides are roughly finished. The front face has a framed epigraphic field filling the whole face. The top, above the frame, is entirely lost. The central section above the moulding is 54.8 cm W x 40 D, and the epigraphic field 75.3 H x 48 W.
