Text based upon photograph, after Korhonen

English translation

(tomb) of Gaius Pompeius Felix and Scribonia Restituta

commentary

Given that both names reflect senatorial families attested in the area, this is assumed to be a freedman and freedwoman of the families. Korhonen notes the absence of 'Dis Manibus' as encouraging a first-century date, alongside the palaeography. Castelli and Ferrara report the right fragment only as a separate text, in addition to reporting the entire text, following a notice in Amico.
