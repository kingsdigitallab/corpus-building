English translation

The three units of infantry who served at the time of Apollonios son of Apollonios, and the ephebes of his year (honour) Antallos Ornichas, son of Antallos, grandson of Antallos who held the office of gymnasiarch, for the sake of good will.

Inscription

Layout
Seven lines of Greek letters, filling the face, with a regular left margin, the final line approximately centred; the text is engraved by means of individual dots punched with a rounded tool.
Text condition
No data
Lettering
Regular neatly cut letters, with pronounced terminations to the strokes. Alpha has broken bar; beta a much smaller upper eye, just closed; epsilon has nearly equal strokes; zeta formed by vertical with horizontal top and bottom; kappa has shortened arms; mu has midstrokes descending only half-way, curved rather than angular, and not always reaching the top of the verticals; xsi formed by three horizontals only, the middle one shorter; omicron is full size; pi has horizontal extending beyond the verticals; sigma has horizontal top and bottom strokes; phi is standard height, with ovoid eye; omega is closed, and tear-drop shaped.
