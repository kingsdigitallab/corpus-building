Apparatus criticus

Text of IGPalermo controlled against photograph

English translation

The three units of infantry who served at the time of Apollonios son of Apollonios, and the ephebes of his year (honour) Antallos Ornichas, son of Antallos, grandson of Antallos who held the office of gymnasiarch, for the sake of good will.
