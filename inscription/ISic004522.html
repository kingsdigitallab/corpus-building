commentary

There may be a short vacat after the M. The inscription was recorded by Orsi in his taccuini, but was not mentioned by either Schubring or Mommsen in their records of the obverse (ISic000418). 'DEM' is perhaps most likely part of a form of 'isdem', but there are many other possibilities.
