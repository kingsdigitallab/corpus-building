Apparatus criticus

line.6: The top of the B is visible above the break. The final two Es on this line appear either partially or entirely to lack the lowermost stroke.

English translation

To the shades of the departed. This memorial of Cesernius Marcelus his wife and sons set up for a well deserving man, who lived 43 (?) years ...

commentary

The fragment recorded by Orsi from the lower left recorded an initial X to the numeral and three letters in a 10th line below, which are not easily resolved in a common abbreviation but likely to pertain to the erection of the memorial by Cesernius Marcelus' family. The position of the X at the start of line 9, with at least a partial vacat of one character implied before it in the drawing by Orsi leaves it slightly uncertain exactly what the total age in years recorded was, with a minimum of 33 and a maximum of 53, but 43 seeming the most likely from the spacing on the fragments preserved. The repetition of X across line-break in both uxor and vixit is notable.
