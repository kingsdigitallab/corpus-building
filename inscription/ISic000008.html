Text from photograph; Bivona: Vibia Erothine

English translation

To the shades of the departed, to Vibia Pothine. Vibia Euphrosyne, daughter, for her most dutiful mother and Theophanes for his deserving wife.

commentary

Raepsaet-Charlier corrected the erroneous reading of Bivona of line 2.
