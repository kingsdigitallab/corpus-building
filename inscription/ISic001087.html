Description
Large block of yellow tuff, broken in 8 pieces when found, subsequently restored and set together in plaster. Most of the block is preserved, damaged at the left and right ends, with some of the left half of the face and lower edge missing. The inscribed field (108 cm wide) is cut back from the right and left margins; the upper surface is finished with traces of clamp holes for the fitting of a block above. The rear is uneven.
