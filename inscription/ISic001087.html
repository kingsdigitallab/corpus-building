  • Text of IGPalermo

English translation

It is through the gods listed here that the Selinuntines win victory. Through Zeus we conquer and through Phobos and through Herakles and through Apollo and through Poseidon and through the Tyndaridai and through Athena and through Malophoros and through Pasikrateia and through the other gods, but through Zeus above all. Since there is friendship (it is resolved), that having forged in gold, and having engraved these names, to dedicate in the Apollonion, having written the name of Zeus in first place; and that the gold be sixty talents.

Physical description

Support

Description
Large block of yellow tuff, broken in 8 pieces when found, subsequently restored and set together in plaster. Most of the block is preserved, damaged at the left and right ends, with some of the left half of the face and lower edge missing. The inscribed field (108 cm wide) is cut back from the right and left margins; the upper surface is finished with traces of clamp holes for the fitting of a block above. The rear is uneven.
