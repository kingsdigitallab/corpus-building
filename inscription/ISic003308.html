Text based on photograph; Korhonen: [--- τελε]υτᾷ

English translation

… He/She died …

Italian translation

… Morì …

commentary

This inscription is a fragment of a Christian epitaph, probably with the indication of the date of death introduced by the present indicative of τελευτάω. The letters at l. 1 could be part of the name of the deceased.
