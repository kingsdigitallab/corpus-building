τᾶς Καλικράτεος Μεννάδος ε̄̓μί

Apparatus criticus

Text after Arena 1989 (IGASM I) ; Tusa: Καλικράτεος Μένναρός ε̄̓μι. ; Villa: Μενναδο͂ς.

English translation

I am (the krater) of Mennados (son) of Kallikratos.

Italian translation

Sono (il cratere) di Mennados (figlio) di Kallikratos.

commentary

The first mu (initial of the name Μεννάδος) seems to have a very different ductus from the second one (in ε̄̓μί): the difference could be explained, according to Arena, by assuming an intervention by the writer who would have written a pi at first and then amended it to mu. The name Μεννάδος is not elsewhere attested.
