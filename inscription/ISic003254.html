D(is) M(anibus)
Rubriae Sozusae
vix(it) an(nis) XXX
M(arcus) Refrius Dubitatus
5uxori karissimae

Apparatus criticus

Korhonen 2004

English translation

To the shades of the underworld. For Rubria Sozusa who lived for 30 years. Marcus Refrius Dubitatus for his most dear wife.
