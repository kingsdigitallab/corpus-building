Apparatus criticus

Text from autopsy; Traces of three letters, blue, then red, then blue, are visible at the start of line 1.

translation

commentary

The block was found on top of the dividing wall between tombs 131 and 132, to be understood as epitymbion for the two tombs. A second small fragmentary block, from the same context, with a blue painted (but not engraved) letter may form part of the same or an associated incription (Fragment b)
