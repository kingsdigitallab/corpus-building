Text after Arena (IGASM 2)

English translation

One drachma and four obols: (price of) 120 lekythoi.

Italian translation

Una dracma e quattro oboli: (prezzo di) 120 lekythoi (transl. by Arena 2002).

commentary

Inscription in clear letters under the foot of an intact pelike, depicting a scene with Dionysus and a satyr. Arena (see translation, which we accept) assumes a correspondence between the two values.
