Physical description

Support

Description
Base of brown tuff, broken into many fragments and reconstructed. Upper portion only survives, lower part lost on all sides. Square in form, with one face inscribed. A simple moulding/cornice projects above the face, surrounding the top on all sides. A substantial irregular hollow (4cm deep) on the upper face shows where the dedication once stood on top.
