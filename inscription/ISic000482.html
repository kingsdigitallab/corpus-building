text of CIL

Italian translation

All'imperatore Caesare Traiano Adriano Augusto, figlio del divo Traiano Partico, pontefice massimo, nella sua terza tribunicia potesta, console per la terza volta. Luogo concesso per decreto dei decurioni.

English translation

For the emperor Caesar Trajan Hadrian Augustus, son of the deified Trajan Parthicus, chief priest, holder of the tribunician powers for the third time, consul for the third time. The place was given by decree of the town councillors.
