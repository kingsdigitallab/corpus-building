Apparatus criticus

  • preliminary text following Sciacca 1982-1983;
  • 16: Sciacca reads the final 5 characters as poorly formed Hebrew letters
  • 23: penultimate symbol is a crossed circle
  • 24: final symbol is a rectangle with horizontal crossbar
  • 28: Sciacca reads the final 9 characters as poorly formed Hebrew letters (line misnumbered as 29 in Sciacca's edition)
