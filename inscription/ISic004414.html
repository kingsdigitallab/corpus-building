Lettering

Plain letters carved in a deep square cut: E has shorter mid-bar; upsilon slightly exceeds the line; pi is wide and square in form, with fractionally shorter right hasta; omicron is only slightly smaller than the other letters; mu has vertical first and last strokes, while the mid-strokes descend less than half-way.
