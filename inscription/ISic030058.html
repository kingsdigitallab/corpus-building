English translation

...--as son of Nik-- bought...from Kalikrateia the daughter of Aischylos. Guarantors: Nikias son of Sosias thirteenth (phratry), Saturos son of Ariston thirteenth (phratry), Philandros(?) son of Glaukias fourth (phratry)-??? (tribe), Philonidas(?) son of Herakleidas thirteenth (phratry), Damatrios son of Krithon seventh (phratry)-second (tribe), Philodoros son of Krithon seventh (phratry)-second (tribe).

commentary

With this document we have yet another female seller of property who may be, conservatively, the daughter of Aischylos, or more likely, his widow, depending on restorations in empty spaces.
