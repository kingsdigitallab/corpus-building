Lettering
Relatively broad brushstrokes with some fluidity. Alpha is broad with straight bar; beta has distinct closed eyes, somewhat angular; eta is broad with outward turning lower strokes; theta is ovid with horizontal bar; mu is broad with vertical uprights, and the middle strokes only descending half-way; omicron is sometimes slightly smaller and above the line; sigma is lunate; omega is lunate.
