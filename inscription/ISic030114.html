commentary

The staff is reported as having been found in the territory of Villadoro, near Nicosia; but as Manganaro observes, no conclusion can be drawn from this regarding the original location of Ichana (=Ichaninos / Ichaninon / Sichana / Sichaninos / Sichaninon), which is otherwise only attested in Steph. It is not clear from the photograph and reported readings whether the text on side C is thought to have been erased or simply worn away; given the layout, it does not seem likely that the text on sides B or D included the second word.
