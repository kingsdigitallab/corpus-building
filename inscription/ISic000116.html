  • Text of ILTermini

English translation

Musice, from Vibo (Valentia), aged 26

Physical description

Support

Description
A small limestone stele, rounded at the top, somewhat chipped around the edges, and broken off across the base. The face is only roughly chiselled flat, rather than finished. The rear is unfinished.

commentary

The name is assumed to be a female Greek name, with the Greek nominative ending in -e (Manganaro however considered the name to be masculine).
