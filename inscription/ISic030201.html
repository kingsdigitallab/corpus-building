commentary

This document is one of a large deposit (c. 150) of lead tesserae found in a storeroom of Athena's sanctuary at the archaeological site of Kamarina. The partially legible text ΤΑΚ[.]ΔΟΣ placed just below line 1 on face b is by another hand and relates to an earlier use of the tessera.
