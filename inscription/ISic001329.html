Time and Poim--- made (this) for themselves and their relatives.

Italian translation

Time e Poim--- realizzarono (questo) per loro stessi e per i loro familiari.

commentary

The structure of the epitaph is influenced by Latin epigraphy (fecit sibi et suis) and recurs often (but not exclusively) in Christian epitaphs: for this reason, Ferrua believed that the epitaph was Christian.
