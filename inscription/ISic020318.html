Inscription

Layout
The text consists of two inscriptions, arranged on two different sides of the same object. The first one (face a) is on the underside of the foot: it is arranged in a clockwise spiral, on one line. The second one (face b) is on the underside of the foot: it is arranged in a clockwise spiral, on one line. On both faces the text has been highlighted with white painting in modern times to make it more legible.
