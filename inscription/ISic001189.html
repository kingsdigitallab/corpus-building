ISic001189. Haluntium honours the son of Timandros draft

View in current site

( vac. 4) ὁ δᾶμος ( vac. 3)
Β̣ίωτον Τιμάνδρο[υ]
εὐνοίας ἕνεκεν̣

Apparatus criticus

Text based upon autopsy; Salinas: [Κ]ίωτον; Kaibel: [Β]ίωτον; lapis: trace of upper eye of Β (contra Salinas, trace of K).

Italian translation

Il damos (onora) Biotos figlio di Timandros per benevolenza

English translation

The people (honours) Biotos son of Timandros, for the sake of goodwill

commentary

Notwithstanding the damage, it is clear that there was no ethnic included in the first part of the text, and the inscription appears complete. The name Βίωτος is also attested on tile and amphora stamps from southern Italy; no other form ending -ιωτος is known from the region.
