commentary

The recess in the stone may have been cut to replace a Greek inscription with an Oscan one, which Crawford (2011) believes suggests a date soon after the Mamertine takeover of Messana, i.e. c. 275 BC.

The inscription is cut into the stucco and painted red. Much of the bottom of the stucco has been damaged. ‚ÄúIt is highly likely that there was a third line in the panel, and quite possible that there were further lines on a block below.‚Äù (Crawford 2011)
