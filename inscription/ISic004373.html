Lettering
The letters are well cut in a relatively refined v-cut, with modest serifs. The letters are tend towards the tall and narrow and are very regular and evenly spaced. P and R are open, O is somewhat ovoid, E has bars of equal length, D is quite full, the diagonal of the N does not always start from the top of the first hasta. Interpuncts are not consistently used, with one elegant example in line 2
