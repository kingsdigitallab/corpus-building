English translation

Gamike, worthy and blameless, farewell! She lived 26 years.

commentary

The name Gamike is also attested in imperial period funerary texts from Katana and Lipara. There is no clear basis for determining the potential relationship between Kopria, Mousa and Gamike, except that it seems likely that Mousa was the child of one of the other two.
