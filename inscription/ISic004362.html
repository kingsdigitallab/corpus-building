Apparatus criticus

text from autopsy; Manganaro suggests: [桅维]位位慰 or [苇]位位慰
2: Manganaro suggests: [蔚]喂委位[慰 (?)]

translation

commentary

It is a reasonable assumption that the text is a simple funerary inscription, containing the name and patronymic of the deceased; it may have been accompanied by one or more formulaic elements such as 伪喂蔚.
