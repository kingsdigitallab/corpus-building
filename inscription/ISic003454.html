Apparatus criticus

Text of Rizzone 2009 5: μαν Rizzone 2009 suggests as alternative

Italian translation

Qui giace Iohannes monaco, Menouth (o Mamanth).

English translation

Here lies Iohannes Menouth (or Mamanth), the monk.

commentary

The text of Cordano 2005 / 2014 is misleading (and the suggestion that the text is in a mixture of Greek and Latin is clearly incorrect), and the edition of Rizzone is to be preferred.
