Apparatus criticus

Text of Agnello ; In the drawing, NTA is clear, and alternative interpretations (a larger number in the tens) should be considered

English translation

Felix, worthy and blameless, lived 7 years, 2 months five days. Farewell. ...

commentary

The letters in the final line are larger, but it is not immediately clear from the drawing that they must be in a second hand (the assumption of Agnello is that they were written later). Judgement on this point, and the preferred reading of line 3 should be reserved until autopsy is possible.
