Lettering
Deeply incised letters, slightly elongated. Alpha is extended at the top, with straight bar; epsilon is in form of a four-bar sigma with middle bar extended to match top and bottom strokes; zeta is regular Z; theta rhomboid with full middle bar; kappa has full length arms; mu has all strokes off vertical, with the middle strokes not fully descending; omicron is rhomboid; pi has overextended crossbar; rho has rounded and closed head; sigma is regular four-bar; omega is rhomboid but open at the base.
