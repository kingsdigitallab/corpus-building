English translation

Shipowners. Olympis son of Hyperbolos; Theugnis son of Eubios; Phryneidas son of Teisandros; Diokles son of...; Aristodamos son of Eubios; Ogeaokos(?) son of Eupheide (?); ...son of Peitharchos; Peisandros son of Agathon; ... son of Teisandros; Apollodoros son of Archidamos; Zopuros son of Naukrates; ...son of Uperbolos; Aischron son of Aristoxenos. To Aphrodite.
