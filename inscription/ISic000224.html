ISic000224. Funerary epitaph for L. Tennius Herma

Latin, funerary, plaque

edited, View in current site

1DISMAN
2LTENNIUS
3HERMA
4Vivy-leafAivy-leafLXXV

Apparatus criticus

Text of autopsy

English translation

To the Shades of the Underworld. Lucius Tennius Herma lived for 75 years.

commentary

The cognomen, Herma, of Greek origin, is extremely common. By contrast, the gens Tennius is unique, although it is similar to the otherwise attested Stennius, Vettennius, Maetennius (etc).
