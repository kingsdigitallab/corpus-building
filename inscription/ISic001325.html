Apparatus criticus

Text based on photographs; Korhonen observes that the name of the deceased could be in the nominative form, but the presence of the attribute suggests that it is dative.

English translation

To the sweetest Sosia Elate, Aphrodeisios, her husband.

Italian translation

Alla dolcissima Sosia Elate, Aphrodisios, il marito.

commentary

The epitaph presents a less common structure, with the name of the deceased probably in dative and the name of the dedicant in nominative followed by an attribute that qualifies his relationship with the deceased (see Korhonen 2004: 87).
