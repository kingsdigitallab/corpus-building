Λικίνις Ἰσαυ-
ρος ζῶν ὢν
ἐποίει ἑαυτῷ
καὶ τοῖς ἰδίοις

Apparatus criticus

Text after Zavettieri 2017

English translation

Likinis Isauros made this while still living for himself and his family

Italian translation

Licinio Isauro mentre era in vita faceva per se stesso e per i suoi familiari
