Inscription

Layout
The text consists of two inscriptions, arranged on two different parts of the same object. The first one (fragment a) is on the underside of the foot: it is arranged in a clockwise spiral, on two lines. The second one (fragment b) is on the bottom of the interior of the cup and is arranged following a linear pattern on four lines.
