commentary

This document is one of a large deposit (c. 150) of lead tesserae found in a storeroom of Athena's sanctuary at the archaeological site of Kamarina. l. 3 on face a can be read by turning the tessera upside down, it is written in smaller characters, and corresponds to the registration of the name on face b. This is, according to the editor, a double use of the tessera by the same person. For a similar reuse of tesserae see also ISic030197, ISic030198, and ISic030211.
