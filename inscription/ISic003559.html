πάσῃ [.] ἀρετῇ [...] σοφία [..] καὶ ΤΗ ΠΙΤΑΝΗ
Κλαυδίου [..] Γαυου
ἐποίησε καὶ ἐνεωτέ[ρισε]
5μνήμης χάριν

Apparatus criticus

Text based on comparison of Kaibel, Filippi and SEG, prioritising Kaibel's transcription of the manuscript tradition.; Kaibel notes that the likely source text, IG XIV.282, has ΕΠΙ ΤΑΜΙΑ misread as ΤΗ ΠΙΤΑΝΗ by Torremuzza

translation

commentary

As Korhonen lucidly details, the inscription is one of a series of late 18th century local antiquarian forgeries, constructed on the basis of existing texts, principally those to be found in the 1769 first edition of the corpus of Torremuzza. Kaibel classed the entire series already as forgeries in IG XIV.
