Description
A thick rectangular slab of the local breccia di San Marco (a pink/red stone with white veins), broken in the upper right corner. There is a circular round hole in the top surface.

commentary

One of two inscriptions on this upright stone, broken in its upper right area. The other inscription, on the front face upper left is ISic001412. Cf. IG XIV.369-71 for other evidence of gymnasion in Haluntium. The text of Manganaro is ambitious and requires revision in light of fresh autopsy
