Lettering
(Following Orsi's apograph in his taccuino) Small regular neat letters with terminations to the strokes but no signficant serifs. Alpha has straight bar; Epislon has slightly shortened middle bar; Zeta is vertical with wide top and bottom horizontal strokes; Kappa has slightly shortened arms; Mu has vertical first and last, with the middle strokes almost reaching to the base line; omicron is full-size; Sigma has parallel top and bottom strokes; Omega is round full-sized, and almost closed.

commentary

The text is recorded by Orsi in his fourth notebook in 1889, inserted into his NSA publication of the same year. It has not, so far, been relocated in the museum. Manganaro suggests this is a list of Ephebes, but although it is clearly a list of names, more than that cannot said.
