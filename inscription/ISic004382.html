Apparatus criticus

Text after Zavettieri 2017 and photograph

English translation

Rufina Marcia having lived for 35 years, her husband Sossis made this memorial for his own most pious wife

Italian translation

Rufina Marcia vissuta anni 35, il marito Sossis fece alla propria moglie piissima un monumento

commentary

Cf. ISic003521 for another female member of the gens Marcia in Messina. The rare name Sossis is also attested at Catania in ISic001324
