Lettering

Simply and neatly cut letters with almost no serifs, well-spaced horizontally, but with minimal interlinear spacing. Simple interpuncts used frequently but inconsistently. Alpha has broken bar; epsilon is rectilinear with equal length bars; zeta is simple Z; theta is rhomboid with detached cross-bar; kappa has slightly shortened arms; omicron is rhomboid; rho has small closed and rounded eye; sigma is rectilinear with horizontal top and bottom strokes.
