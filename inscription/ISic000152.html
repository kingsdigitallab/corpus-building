Lettering
The text is irregularly and shakily carved. Letter height and width differ even on the same line, and the T on line 2 is significantly taller than other letters. Simple serifs used. The vertical bar for the 'R' in line 2 is bisected by a long horizontal foot. At the end of line 2, the top of a serifed downstroke is just visible after the T.

commentary

The lack of a praenomen on the stone means that it is most likely that the epitaph was for a woman, most plausibly called Claudia Fortunata, but as Bivona observes there are instances of male individuals lacking a praenomen in the Termini corpus also (e.g. ISic000153), so this is hardly conclusive.
