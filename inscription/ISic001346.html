English translation

Titus Aelius Victorinus lived 16 years, 5 months, 17 days. His dutiful brothers made (this) together with their mother from the legacy of Victorinus ...

Italian translation

Titos Aelios Victorinos visse 18 anni, 5 mesi e 17 giorni. I suoi fratelli pietosi hanno realizzato (questo) insieme con la loro madre dagli averi di Vittorino ...

Physical description

Support

Description
Fragment of the lower part of a marble relief, probably belonging to a sarcophagus, with inscribed text on the moulding. The rear is smooth, with rough border projecting at the base. The feet of a person or a horse are preserved of the relief above the inscription. The surface is somewhat worn.
