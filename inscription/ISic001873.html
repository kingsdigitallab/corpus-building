ISic001873. Funerary inscription for Kopria edited

View in current site

Κοπρία
χρηστὰ ((ivy-leaf)) καὶ
ἄμεμπτε
χαῖρε ((ivy-leaf)) ἔζη-
5σεν ἔτη λ´

Apparatus criticus

Text from autopsy; Orsi: ΚΟΠΡΙΑ(Ι)
5: Orsi: ΕΤΗΑ.

English translation

Kopria, worthy and blameless, farewell. She lived 30 years.

commentary

The name Kopria is rare, with only a single Sicilian attestation, from Catania. There is no clear basis for determining the potential relationship between Kopria, Mousa and Gamike, except that it seems likely that Mousa was the child of one of the other two.
