Support

Description
A funerary altar, somewhat eroded on all faces. Quadrangular in form, with triple moulding at the top of the main block, surmounted by scrolls on each side with a central horn, and a raised square block on the upper surface behind the horn. The front face of the altar has relief designs on the lower half, which may relate to milling, and a relief form of a tabula ansata on the upper part, which contains a Greek funerary inscription. The right side carries further relief designs showing tools associated with crafts.
