ISic001127. I.Sicily inscription 001127 edited

View in current site

Βέττιος Μαύ-
ρικος ἐτρῶν
ι´ ἔζησεν δὲ τὸ-
ν ἑαυτοῦ βείο[ν]
5κῖτε ἐν τούτ-
ῳ τῷ τύ((ν))βῳ

Apparatus criticus

Text based upon the engraving prepared by Castelli, Principe di Torremuzza; Kaibel proposes Π for the age, but it is not clear on what basis; as Castelli observes, iota is clear; Muratori reported Κ

English translation

Bettios Maurikos lived his own life for 10 years, he lies in this tomb

commentary

Muratori's edition on three lines is condemned by Castelli, Principe di Torremuzza, and the latter's engraving forms the basis for the current edition. The inscription was transferred to the Museum of San Martino, but does not seem to have passed from there to the Palermo museum and is now lost.
