Text: Manganaro 1989

English translation

To Sextus Nonius Africanus, son of Sextus, of tribe Quirina [...] lived for 28 years. To Caecilia Zotica, Sextus Nonius Albanus made this for his excellent wife. One sestertius per person [was provided] for a banquet worthy of her.
