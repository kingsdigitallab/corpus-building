Δεινίας Κηφ[ισο]δότου Βουτάδης
θεσμοθετήσας ἐπὶ Φειδοστράτου
ἄρχοντος ἀνέθηκεν
[ἡ βουλή] ὁ δῆμος

Apparatus criticus

Text of IG

English translation

Deinias son of Kephisodotos of Boutadai, who was thesmothete when Pheidostratos was archon, dedicated this. (In crown, on the left) The Council. (In crown, on the right) The People.

Italian translation

Deinias figlio di Kephisodotos di Boutadai, che era tesmoteta durante l'arcontato di Pheidostratos, dedicò questo. (In corona, a sinistra) Il consiglio. (In corona, a destra) Il popolo.
