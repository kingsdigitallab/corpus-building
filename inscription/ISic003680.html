translation

commentary

Without further information, such as the dimensions of the letters, it is impossible to know whether these fragments relate to e.g. a building inscription or a more ephemeral text, but the fact that they are described as both incised and painted, encourages a view that they are more formal, while the brief description of form perhaps suggests a later rather than earlier date.
