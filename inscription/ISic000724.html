translation

commentary

Manganaro proposed expanding to [C. Iulio Caesari] imp(eratori) Div[i f(ilio) / [? triumviro r.p.c. st]atua dic(ata), although there is no obvious parallel for 'imp(erator) div(i [filius]' (Manganaro points to ILS 76 and 8893). The form of the letters is notable (implying that they were filled with a material). The text has not yet been located, but a photograph taken by Manganaro in the 1960s is preserved and here reproduced.
