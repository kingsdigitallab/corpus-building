text of CIL

English translation

A copy of his will: Greetings, master and father, and farewell, master and father. When I dictated these words to you, I considered you the most miserable person, as you were, when you sent me to this place. I request that you build a monument to me worthy of my youth. I request you to manumit my ward Eutychianus and free him from his bonds, and also my slave Apriles, who is the only survivor of those in my service. Written at Sirmium on the 15th day before the Kalends of April [18 March], during the consulship of L. Calpurnius Piso and P. Salvius Iulianus [175 CE]
