Apparatus criticus

Text after Manganaro 1994 but controlled with autopsy/photograph; Manganaro: ιβ’

English translation

Here lies Nonios Pi[--], a carpenter. A column and Fate snatched away this once most handsome man, who left behind the most wretched grief for his father. He lived 22 years.

commentary

A verse distych begins midway through line 3.
