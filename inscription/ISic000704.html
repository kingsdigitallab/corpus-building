D (is) M (anibus) s (acrum)

Apparatus criticus

English translation

Sacred to the spirits of the underworld. Gaius Iulius Eutychus lived for 40 years. His wife made this for a well deserving man.

Italian translation

Consacrato agli Dei Mani | Caius Iulius Eutychus | Visse per 40 anni | La moglie pose per (il marito) meritevole
