Apparatus criticus

  • Text based upon the apograph in Marconi 1926; Marconi presents several of the letters 'reversed', but given that the orientation of a mason's mark need not follow the final orientation of the stone, this does not seem significant;
  • e.1: The symbol has the form of an hourglass
  • i.1: The symbol has the form of an incomplete (upper left) and open (at the waist) hourglass

Description
The individual blocks of the sima were observed by Marconi to be countersigned by letters (or numbers), of which he transcribed a number of examples without further information.
