Large rounded cippus of pale-grey stone. Broken right, rounded top. Tapers both left and right to the base. Bottom edge is original. The rear was inaccessible during autopsy.

Lettering
Text is deeply and broadly incised into the stone, except for the interpuncts. Letters do not uniformly follow the line, and are of varying sizes. O at end of line 2 is much smaller than the rest of the text. Spacing is inconsistent, with the Xs on line three touching. Light serifs used on letters.
