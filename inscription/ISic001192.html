ISic001192. I.Sicily inscription 001192

Facsimile Images

οἱ ἀλειφόμε[νοι]
Ἀγάθαρχον
Ἡρακλείου

Apparatus criticus

Text according to Kaibel in IG ; Gualtherus: ΑΝΑΟΑΡΧΟΝ. Bartholini: ΑΝΑΣΙΑΡΧΟΝ. Wilamowitz: Ἀνά[ξ]αρχον.

English translation

The annointed (honour) Agatharchos, son of Herakleios.

commentary

The stone was seen by Gualtherus, but (contra Fuduli 2017: 176, who seems to misread Salinas' report in NSA 1880, pp. 191-195, referring a description of IG XIV.370 to this text), was no longer discoverable when Salinas visited in 1880.
