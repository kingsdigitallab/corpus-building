English translation

--- for him a grave --- / --- in this tomb / --- in accordance with his dipositions / --- he put round himself a crown / --- a gift to the son.

Italian translation

--- per lui una tomba --- / --- in questo sepolcro / --- da sue diposizioni / --- si cinse di una corona / --- un dono al figlio.
