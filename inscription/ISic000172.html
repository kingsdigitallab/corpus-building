Apparatus criticus

Text from autopsy

English translation

To the Shades of the Underworld. Eunus lived 16 years.

commentary

The name Eunus is familiar in Sicily from the name of the leader of the first Sicilian slave revolt (135-132 BCE), but is otherwise unattested on the island.
