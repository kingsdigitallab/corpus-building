Physical description

Support

Description
Fragment of a thick plaque or stele of limestone. Intact on the left and part of the front and rear face, but broken top, bottom, and on the right. A substantial raised moulding is partially preserved along the left side, which could originally have been a relief carving of an engaged column, but has a flat / smooth front survace. Part of the upper right of the preserved face is also lost.

Inscription

Layout
Remains of 16 densely packed lines of Greek, with the left margin intact; the faint suggestion of a raised border in the lower left corner implies that the final line preserved may be the final line of the text.
