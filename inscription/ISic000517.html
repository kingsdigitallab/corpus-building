Apparatus criticus

Text: CIL

English translation

To Marcus Marcius Bietis Glaucus, son of Marcus, of tribe Claudia, father of a senator, honoured with the public horse and patron on account of his merits which he conferred upon his fellow citizens, vir clarissimus, adlected amongst the tribunes by the Imperator Caesar Marcus Aurelius Commodus Antoninus Pius Felix Augustus, the tribesmen of tribe Jupiter Augustus [gave this] with their own money. This place was given at public expense by decree of the decurions.
