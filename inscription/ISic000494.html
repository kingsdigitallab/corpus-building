Text after CIL

English translation

To the spirits of the dead. Lucius Metilius Proculus made (this) for Claudia Sabina, dearly beloved spouse.

Italian translation

Agli spiriti dei defunti. Lucius Metilius Proculus fece (questo) per Claudia Sabina, amatissima consorte.
