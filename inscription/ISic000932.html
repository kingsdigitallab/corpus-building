Apparatus criticus

Text based upon Orsi and Ferrua; Δ(αιμοσιν) Θ(εοῖς) Χ(θονίοις)Ferrua; [Ἐνθά]δε κ[ῖτε]KaibelOrsi and Crispi report three characters, seemingly a lambda, Roman D or a theta, and a chi, at the centre of the line.
4: ICCrispiKaibel proposes reading IE after Crispi
5: Earlier editors propose reading ΚΑΛ before the month name, but Ferrua is correct in suggesting there is no space and it was probably omitted.
