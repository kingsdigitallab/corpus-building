Description
A thick rectangular slab of the local breccia di San Marco (a pink/red stone with white veins), broken in the upper right corner. There is a circular round hole in the top surface.

Lettering

Letters of the mid-Hellenistic period: epsilon has short middle bar; omicron is small and mid-line; mu has vertical first and last strokes, the mid-point descends half-way; alpha is formed with both broken and straight bar; sigma has approximately parallel top and bottom strokes; omega is open; theta formed with a central point.
