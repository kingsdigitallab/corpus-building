English translation

To Marcus Arruntius Brocch[us]. (This) burial plot was granted, at public expense, by the decree of the town council. In width [...] feet, in depth 20(+?) feet.

Physical description

Support

Description
A stele or cippus with a rounded upper edge, of a dark grey limestone. Intact on the left, and on the left lower edge and probably the upper left edge also; but broken/damaged on the right upper and lower parts. The face is partly flaked off the surface, and a natural flaw runs vertically across the face of the stone also.
