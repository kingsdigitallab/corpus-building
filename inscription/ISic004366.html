Apparatus criticus

Text from autopsy; Orsi: ἔζησε σ[εμνῶς]

English translation

Roscius Magnus, a good man, farewell. You lived [--] years...

commentary

The parallel offered by ISic004364 and ISic004365 suggests that ἔζησες ἔ[τη] is more plausible as a restoration in lines 4-5 than Orsi's proposal of ἔζησε σ[εμνῶς].
