Italian translation

Euskia l'incensurabile, che visse onestamente e nobilmente anni piu' o meno 25, mori' nella festa della signora mia Lucia, per la quale non e' necessario pronunciare encomio. Cristiana fedele (e) perfetta, gradita al proprio marito per le (sue) molte grazie, affabile

Physical description

Support

Description
A marble plaque, approximately square in form, intact on left and upper edges, but irregular on right and broken along the lower edge towards the right hand side. The plaque had plaster on both front and rear when found, from later re-use as a tomb cover.
