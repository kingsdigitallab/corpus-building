Apparatus criticus

Previous editions are Manganaro's ed. pr. in PP 1989, and Dubois in IGDS II no. 56. This text and the apparatus are copied from Souza 2022 in ZPE; [Ἐπὶ---], Manganaro. Neither M. nor Dubois read any letters on my Line 1, and M. inserts an additional unrestored line (dashes) between my Lines 1 and 2; [τὸ ἥμισσον τᾶ]ς οἰκίας, M. and D. but no sigma is visible on the sheet; πέ[ντε ταλάντων?], M.
3: [Ἄμποχοι] Παντίας, M. and D.; Λυκίσκου Λ̣, M.
4: Φυ̣λα̣κ[ός?], M. The upsilon after the phi is unusual: it is reversed compared to the other upsilons, i.e. the short part is on the right; also the more vertical hasta is extremely long and runs down into the next line.
5: [---]Φιλωνίδας[---], M. and D.
6: [---]ΑΣΙΔΑΣ̣Λ ++ ? ++ Τ[---], M.

English translation

When Theon was in office...Pantime, daughter of Lykiskos...buys five houses...Phinton, son of Lykiskos, guardian...Sosias, Philonidas...Herakleidas, Antallos...

commentary

The engraving of this brief contract is extremely light and difficult to read. The list of names at the end, headed by the brother of the buyer (who is designated as the "guardian" of his sister, who could not transact business independently), likely comprises the guarantors of the contract.
