Apparatus criticus

Text based on photograph; Castelli read the text without the lacuna, but according to Korhonen, this is a not recorded supplement by the previous editors

English translation

Katylleinos lived for 13 years. Eudoia made (this) for his memory

Italian translation

Katylleinos visse per 13 anni. Euodia fece (questo) in sua memoria.
