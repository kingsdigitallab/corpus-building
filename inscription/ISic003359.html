Lettering
Somewhat irregular sized letters, not strictly following the horizontal. Pronounced but simple heavy serifs on deep v-cut letters. Alpha has extended top stroke, with cross-bar sloping down to the left; epsilon is lunate; kappa has short equal length arms; mu has four equal slopint strokes; rho has a large just closed eye; phi has ovoid body with standard height vertical; omega is cursive.
