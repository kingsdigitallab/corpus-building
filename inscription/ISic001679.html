Text of I.Segesta

English translation

To Zeus. The people of the Segestans (have dedicated this statue of) Herakleios Pe[---] son of Nymphon, on account of his excellence

Italian translation

A Zeus. Il popolo dei Segestani (ha onorator/ha eretto la statua di) Herakleios, figlio di Nymphon, Pe[---], per il suo valore [transl. from I.Segesta]
