English translation

To the Underworld Gods. You know about the flowerful fields of the Cappadocian people. There I was born from good parents. After I left them, I went to the West and to the East. My name is Glaphyr (elegant) and so is my soul. I lived until my sixtieth year being completely free, and knew the beauty of fortune and the bitterness of life.
