English translation

To the shades of the underworld. Cornelius Epaphruitus lived for 8 years.

commentary

Korhonen convincingly makes the case that this is a Sicilian inscription, from Termini, not an Urban inscription from Rome. He additionally notes the rarity of linguistic interference as here, in the Termini Latin inscriptions.
