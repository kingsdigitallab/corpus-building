Description
A white limestone altar, finished with a claw chisel on all four sides, with inscription on the front face, a relief of an eagle standing on a thunderbolt on the reverse, mouldings on all four sides form the base and the upper register. The altar is broken/damaged across the top

Lettering
Carefully carved letters, with minimal use of serifs, most visible on tau and chi; the iota adscript at the end of line 2 is small and mid-line; omicron is rhomboid in form, the hastae of the mu are all angled.
