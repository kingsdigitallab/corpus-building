Lettering
Letters are deeply v-cut, somewhat elongated, and showing some tendency towards curvature in e.g. the M. Minial use of serifs. Lunate omega, but rectilinear epsilon and sigma. Alpha with broken bar; mu with four full-length, slanting strokes; kappa with short ams; omicron ovoid but full height; epsilon tall and thin, all bars of equal length; zeta an equal length Z. Use of interpunct to mark off the names, and hedera around the numeral.
