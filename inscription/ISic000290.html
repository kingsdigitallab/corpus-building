CIL

English translation

Sacred to the gods of the underworld. Eutyches and Coetonis, his parents, [gave this] to their dutiful son, Titus Aelius. He lived for seventeen years, seven months, and twenty days.

commentary

Lacunae are rationalized by Mommsen from Ansaldi's 1851 edition. The latter rendered 'T. Aelio' as IAEIO (line 2) and 'Eutyches et Coetonis parentes' as FVIYCHIS·FICOE / IONIS PARINIES (lines 3 and 4)
