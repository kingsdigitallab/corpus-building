  • Text of IG

English translation

Near these doorposts to the temple of Sarapis, Karneades of Barke, the temple-warden, son of Eukritos, dedicated this sacred altar to Hestia, visitor, together with his wife Pythia and his daughter Erasos. In exchange, you reigning over Zeus' magnificent house, that they may always have well-pleasing happiness of life.
