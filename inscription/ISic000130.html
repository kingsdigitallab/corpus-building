ISic000130. Funerary inscription for Antonius Iulianus draft

View in current site

Antonius Iulianus
v(ixit) a(nnis) XXV

Apparatus criticus

Text of Romano

English translation

Antonius Iulianus lived 25 years.

commentary

There is no reason to doubt the validity of the text and its report in Romano 1838, notwithstanding its omission by Mommsen, which suggests it was no longer on display in the museum in the late 19th century. It has not been seen in the 20th or 21st century.
