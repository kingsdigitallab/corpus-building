commentary

A list of names (nom.), 7 men, 1 possibly a woman, to the left of them the sign >, interpreted by the editors as a γ(ράφο). (Jordan) Plaque de plomb contenant une liste de huit noms gravés négligemment. (Dubois) Μύχα es la unica mujer entre las victimas. La onomástica es griega. (Lopez-Jimeno)
