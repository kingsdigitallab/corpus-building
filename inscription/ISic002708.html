Physical description

Support

Description
Large intact stele, very carefully squared and with smooth surfaces. In the upper half of the front face, it has a wide and shallow hollow, with an arched upper end. Inside there remain traces of applied elements (perhaps stucco), also arched at the top, no longer preserved. The recess measures 44cm x 58cm.
