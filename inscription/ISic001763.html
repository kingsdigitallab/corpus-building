English translation

Dionysia and Pompeia(?), her daughter, (lie) here, departed in god, the mother 18 days before the kalends of October [14 September] in the consulship of Mallios Theodoros [399 CE], the daughter 9 days before the kalends of February [24 January] following the fifth consulship of Arkadios and Honorios, the Augusti [402 CE]

commentary
