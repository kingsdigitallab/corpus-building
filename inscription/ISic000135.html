Physical description

Support

Description
Top part of a plaque of a variegated brown/pink stone. Intact top, left and right (apart from the very top left corner), broken across the lower half. The rear is very uneven, and cut back, from the front edges on all sides, to that the front edge is thin, but mostly preserved. The surface is relatively poor due to the stone's typology.
