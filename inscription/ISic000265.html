English translation

Lucius Baebius Iuncinus, son of Lucius, of tribe Galeria, praefectus fabrum, prefect of the 4th Cohort of Raeti, military tribune of the 22nd Legion of Deitarus, prefect of Ala Astyrum, praefectus vehiculorum, justice-officer of Egypt
