English translation

Sacred to the spirits of the dead. To the very loyal Gallicano, who was estate manager at Afiniana. He lived for 45 years.

Italian translation

Sacro agli spiriti dei defunti. Al fedelissimo Gallicano, che fu sovrintendente ad Afiniana. Visse per 45 anni.
