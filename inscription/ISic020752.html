Apparatus criticus

Text after Martzloff 2011

English translation

translation

[Martzloff's first hypothesis is that it is a ludic text involving one speaker talking to two interlocutors]

(Speaker to Interlocutor 1) [...] You are afar.Come closer, you who wish (sc. drinking)

(Speaker to Interlocutor 2) Eurymachus, come, thirsty, pass the jug (Speaker to Interlocutor 1)

(Speaker to Interlocutor 1) drink!

translation

[Martzloff's second hypothesis is that it is a ludic text involving two speakers]

(Speaker1 to Speaker2) [...] You are afar, come closer, you who want to (sc. drink)

(Speaker2 to Speaker1) Come on, thirsty, pass the jug! / Come on, I drink, pass the jug

(Speaker1 to Speaker2) Drink!

Italian translation

translation

(Speaker to Interlocutor 1) [...] Tu sei lontano. Avvicinati, tu che vuoi (sc. bere)

(Speaker to Interlocutor 2) Eurymachus, vieni, assetato, passa la brocca (Speaker to Interlocutor 1)

(Speaker to Interlocutor 1) Bevi!

translation

(Speaker1 to Speaker2) [...] Tu sei lontano, avvicinati, tu che vuoi (sc. bere)

(Speaker2 to Speaker1) Dai, assetato, passa la brocca! / Dai, bevo, passa la brocca!

(Speaker1 to Speaker2) Bevi!
