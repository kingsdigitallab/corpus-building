Apparatus criticus

Text after comparison of Kaibel, Filippi and SEG, prioritising Kaibel's transcription of the manuscript tradition; Kaibel reports ΤΩΝ with Ο marked above the Ω
7: ΠΠ: perhaps for p(ecunia) p(osuit) or p(ropria) p(ecunia) (so Tybout, ap. SEG)

English translation

To all the Saviour Deities of the city of Drepanon. Lucius Malios, chiliarch, dedicated a table and a pedestal with his own money.

commentary

As Korhonen lucidly details, the inscription is one of a series of late 18th century local antiquarian forgeries, constructed on the basis of existing texts, principally those to be found in the 1769 first edition of the corpus of Torremuzza. Kaibel classed the entire series already as forgeries in IG XIV.
