Apparatus criticus

Text of autopsy; Although Bivona (1994) restores a T as the first visible letter of line 2, the top bar is evidently not wide enough for this to be the case. Evident at the end of the line, and not recorded by Bivona, is another letter, which has a tall, straight letter with serifs, potentially an F.

translation

commentary

In potentially reading [Cest]ia, here, Bivona (1994) places too much weight on seeing the first visible letter of line 2 as a 'T', which does not appear likely.
