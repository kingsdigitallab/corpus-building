Lettering
Neatly painted square letters with short serifs, painted in the style of stone cut letters. Alpha is broad with straight bar; Epsilon has equal length horizontal strokes; theta is ovoid with a full horizontal bar; kappa has arms that are not quite ful length; mu has vertical first and last, with middle strokes nearly reaching down to the line; pi has equal length verticals; sigma is four-bar, of equal length, top and bottom strokes horizontal.
