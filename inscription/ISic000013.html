Text of ILMusPalermo

English translation

To Julia Augusta, (wife) of Emperor Caesar Lucius Septimius Severus Pertinax Augustus Pius, victor in Parthia, Arabia and Parthia Adiabene, pontifex maximus, holding tribunician power for the third time, imperator for the fifth, consul for the second, Father of Fatherland. (Erected by) the citizens of Panhormus.
