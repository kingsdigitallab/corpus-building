Apparatus criticus

Text of Nenci

English translation

[name] calls Tamika his wife

commentary

Nenci observes that the letter forms reflect Elymian types. The 'informal' scratched form of the inscription leaves it uncertain what the status of this text might be in relation to the act of burial. An online edition is also available at https://saet.sns.it/it/antiquarium-giuseppe-nenci/iscrizione-graffita-e3659/.
