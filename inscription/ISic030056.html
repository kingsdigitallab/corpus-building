Apparatus criticus

Text based on Manganaro in Parola del Passato 1989. Neither M. nor Dubois in IGDS2 expand the tribe and phratry designations in the text, though D. does write them out in his French translation.; [Φιλιστίω?]νος, M.

English translation

When Philinos was in office, on the fourth (day) of Dionysios, Pausanias son of --on was president. Herakleidas son of Euthykles second (phratry)-second (tribe) bought half of all the land that had belonged to Nymphodoros in Turakinaia and (half) of everything on the land, being in common and undivided according to Sosigenes and Ariston, for two hundred and fifty talents from Sosigenes son of Apollonidas second (phratry)-second (tribe) and Ariston son of Zoilos second (phratry)-first (tribe). Guarantors: Kreton son of Olympis second (phratry)-second (tribe), Apollonios son of Phintias fourth (phratry)-second (tribe), Prodoxos son of Lykiskos sixth (phratry)-second (tribe), Timokrates son of Sophronidas fourth (phratry)-second (tribe), --on son of Zoilos fifth (phratry)-second (tribe), Philarchos son of Antallos sixth (phratry)-??? (tribe), --on son of Andreas second (phratry)-first (tribe), Hagesandros son of Damarchos second (phratry)-second (tribe), Sannon son of Philon twelfth (phratry)-first (tribe), --on son of Histieios last (phratry)-second (tribe).
