English translation

I have faith that I will rest in the peace of our Lord in this tomb until the day of judgement. (The deceased), a foremost member of the local bureaucracy, was laid to rest in this tomb (...) in the month of November at the age of (?). If anyone should open (?) this tomb or remove my bones at any point in time (...)

commentary

Manganaro comments that deceased was primus notarius (and not primicerius notariorum), i.e. a member of the bureaucracy. He argues that the text follows an erased sign of the cross.
