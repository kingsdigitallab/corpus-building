English translation

Here lies Agathe, of 40 years, (passed away) 7 days before the Kalends of September.

Italian translation

Qui giace Agathe, di 40 anni, (morta) 7 giorni prima delle Kalende di Settembre.

commentary

The structure of the epitaph is frequent in the Christian age. The name of the deceased recurs in some Paleochristian epitaphs from Catania, such as IG 14.524 = ISic001344, IG 14.525 = ISic000964, IG 14.552 = ISic001371. Kirchhoff’s supplement at l. 3 ἐτε]λ[εύτησεν τῇ does not seem to be necessary (moreover, there is not a lacuna at this line): even if the verb τελευτάω is frequent in this formula after the indication of the years and before the indication of the day of death, a similar structure without the verb τελευτάω recurs also in IGCVO 1380 (Rome). The indication of the day of death as dies natalis is common in Christian epitaphs.
