commentary

Inscription of ownership from the sacred area of Sabucina. Arena and Dubois suggest that Τίτᾱ is an anthroponym from the adjective τίτας of Cretan origin (cf. Inscriptiones Creticae IV.15, found in Gortyna, where it is presumably the title of a magistrate).
