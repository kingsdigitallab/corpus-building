Text of I.Segesta

English translation

Artemidora wife of Nymphon dedicated (this statue of) her own father, Artemon son of Aleidas, gymnasiarch, according to the terms of the will.

Italian translation

Artemidora, moglie di Nynmphon, ha dedicato (la statua del) suo proprio padre Artemon figlio di Aleidas, ginnasiarca, per testamento [transl. from I.Segesta]
