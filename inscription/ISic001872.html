English translation

Mousa, worthy and blameless, farewell! She lived 10 months, 25 days.

commentary

The name Mousa is well attested in southern Italy, but rarely in Sicily (at Aitna, Syracusae and Tauromenium). There is no clear basis for determining the potential relationship between Kopria, Mousa and Gamike, except that it seems likely that Mousa was the child of one of the other two.
