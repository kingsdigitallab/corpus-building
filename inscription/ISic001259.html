ὁ δᾶμος τῶν Ταυρομενιτᾶν
Ὄλυμπιν Ὀλύμπιος Μεστὸν
νικάσαντα Πύθια κέλητι
τελείωι

Apparatus criticus

text according to IG.

translation

commentary

The word Μεστὸν might be an abbreviation of a demotic according to SEG 52.936, but is better understood as a second name in the common Sicilian fashion. For the context of his festival participation, see the entry for Olympis Mestis in the Connected Contests project: (Person 1196).
