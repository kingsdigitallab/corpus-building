Apparatus criticus

Text after Manganaro 1994

English translation

Farewell worthy Studiosos! He lived for 60 years after being a pastoral cantor.

Italian translation

Addio, degno Studiosos. Egli visse 60 anni essendo stato un cantore bucolico.
