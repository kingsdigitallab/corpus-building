Provenance

Place of origin
Lilybaeum
Provenance found
Recorded by Gualtherus in front of the house of Antonino Gehenne, subsequently seen by Francesco Tardia (1732-1778) in use on staircase on eastern side of church of S. Matteo, but not found by Mommsen.

Current location

Lost.

Date

Likely erected in 500 CE, consequent upon dowry of Theoderic's sister's marriage with the Vandal king leading to transfer of region of Marsala into Vandal control. (AD 500 - AD 535)
