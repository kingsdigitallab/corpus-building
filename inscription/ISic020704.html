commentary

The text can be interpreted as an Italic bimember onomastic formula (see e.g. ISic020602 and ISic020603). For the apheretic use of -εμι at the end of the onomastic formula see Agostiniani 1988-1989, 195-199; Agostiniani, Cordano 2002, 87-89; Sudano 2015, 702-703; commentary of ISic020739-ISic020745, and ISic020766.
