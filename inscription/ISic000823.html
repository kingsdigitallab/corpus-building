ISic000823. Statue base for Hieron II of Syracuse

Facsimile Images

Βασιλέος ἁγε[μόνος]
̒Ιέρωνος Ἱεροκλέος
Συρακόσιοι θεοῖς πᾶσι

Apparatus criticus

Text of Kaibel (IG); ἁγε[ομένου], Dittenberger

English translation

The Syracusans (dedicate this statue) of the king and hegemon Hieron, son of Hierokles, to all the gods

commentary

As John Ma, Statues and Cities (Oxford 2013) 20 n.27 notes, this is an anomalous instance of an honorific dedication where the honorand is in the genitive
