Μανγάνης
τελευτῆσας
μην̣ὶ Νοβε<μ>βρί-
ῳ ἥρων δέχε-
5μ´ ἐ((christogram))νέα´

Apparatus criticus

Text from Orsi 1893; lapis: ἥρων (Orsi interprets as ἠθῶν - αἰτῶν - ἐτων); Orsi: δέκε = δέκα
4-5: Orsi: μένε α

English translation

Manganes, having died in the month of November at the age of 19, (lies here)

commentary

The reading of the last two lines is far from certain. Orsi notes 'si badi ai numerosi idiotismi di cui l'iscrizione e' infarcita.' Orsi proposes reading 10 years and 1 month, whereas Wessel interprets δεχεμ as Latin decem, with ἐνέα for ἐννέα, which seems marginally more plausible (christograms interrupting a word are fairly frequent).
