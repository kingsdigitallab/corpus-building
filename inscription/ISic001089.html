Physical description

Support

Description
Capital/upper part of a roughly square base, originally supporting an offering (as indicated by the carved hollow in the upper surface). A substantial cornice projects around the upper part of the base. Below on one flat face (W 36 cm) is a three line inscription. The surface was covered with a white stucco, of which traces remain.
