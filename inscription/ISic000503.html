Apparatus criticus

Text from CIL; Gualtherus: LILYBITANOR. ; Mommsen et al.: Lilybitanorum; Gualtherus: ARGR. VP S P

English translation

Lucius Pinarius Rufus, on the occasion of his office of aedile, set up this 'image' of the Genius of the municipality of the Lilybaeans from money of a weight of five units, taken from his own funds
