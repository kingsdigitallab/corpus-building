Apparatus criticus

Text of ILTermini. Lines 4-6 which were read almost entirely by Mommsen. Bivona (1994, 113) asserts that several letters could still 'be glimpsed with extreme difficulty' ('si intravedono con estrema difficolta'), but have now completely disappeared.; Mommsen: 'tori im[p]'
5: Mommsen: 'Anton[ini]'

translation

To Sol Invictus, defender of Imperator Antoninius, Augustus

commentary

Sol Invictus is here described as the defender of Elagabalus, as Augustus (conservatori Augusti). This legend also appears on contemporary coins (e.g. RIC 4.2 n.63).
