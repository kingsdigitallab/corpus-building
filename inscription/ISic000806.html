English translation

(tomb of) Alliena Dorothea

commentary

For the name Alliena/us (attested several times at Lipara), note A. Allienus, legatus of Q. Cicero in Asia in 60, was governor of Sicily 48-46 BC. Dorothea (LGPN V3A: 38315) is not otherwise attested in Sicily.
