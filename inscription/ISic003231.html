ISic003231. Epitaph for Epaphrodeitos

Facsimile Images

Ἐπαφρό<δ>ειτος
ἔζησεν
ἔτη ((·)) ξ´ ((·))

Apparatus criticus

Text based on photograph; lapis: Ἐπαφροειτος

English translation

Epaphrodeitos lived for 60 years.

Italian translation

Epaphrodeitos visse 60 anni.

commentary

Korhonen considers the inscription not urban because of the structure of the epitaph and the palaeography. Moreover, the name of the deceased, Ἐπαφρόδιτος, is highly attested in Sicily (see LGPN 3A: 145) and in another inscription from Catania (Libertini 1937: 78).
