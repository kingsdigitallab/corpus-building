translation

commentary

It is assumed the text continued on a second block, probably below (now lost). Manganaro would restore as "Genio Augusti et Ti. Caesaris d.d.", following CIL 10.7340 and a text known only from Gualtherus (Messana, 1624), p.53, n.356.
