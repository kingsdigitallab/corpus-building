English translation

Markos Bipsanios Zosimos (Marcus Vipsanius), having lived well for 35 years. Farewell passerby.

English translation

Markos Bipsanios Zosimos (Marcus Vipsanius), avendo vissuto bene per 35 anni. Salve, viandante.
