Text after Orsi and Strazzulla

English translation

commentary

Orsi - and Strazzulla after him - assumes an unattested name 'Prianos', but more plausible would be 'Koprianos', which is several times attested in the region, with the first part of the name at the end of line 1. Autopsy is required to clarify lines 3-4.
