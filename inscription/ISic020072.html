Apparatus criticus

Italian translation

commentary

According to Agostiniani (1977) the text consists of numerical symbols that would have the value of one obolus and a half in the system of Epidaurus and Thespies. See for a comparison, as suggested by Agostiniani (1977) Tod 1911-1912, pp.105, 109.
