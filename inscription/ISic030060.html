Text is from Dubois in IGDS2; ὅκ’ ἡ [ἐ]μβασί(ς) ἐσ(τ)ιν, M.

English translation

When Orthon is hiarapolos, on the ???th of Theudaisios: Lyson son of Hippias buys the vineyards and all that goes with the vineyards and two shares [i.e. two-thirds] of the wine press, and half of everything else, of the entire farmstead that is on its foundations and half overthing else inside, except for the sacred things, for 115 drachmai and 21 talents from Theston son of Damarchos and from Saturos son of Damon the orphan-rearers. Guarantors: Nikias son of Kratias, Emmenidas son of Damon, Theudoros son of Eupolemos, Philonidas son of Herakleidas, Hiaron son of Philiarchos, Philiarchos son of Apollonidas, Deinon son of Phintias, Philon son of Emmenidas, Polyxenos son of Emmenidas, Pyrrhias son of Karaikos.
