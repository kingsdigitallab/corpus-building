commentary

Dubois points out that the same inscription is found, in Sicily, on a loom weight now in the Museo Paolo Orsi di Siracusa (Orsi 1906, 679, no. 1, fig. 510 ). The weight of 418 g corresponds to two talents of silver, each weighing 208.8 g (Dubois IGDS no. 152).
