commentary

Oursanos may be the name Ursianus. The military unit 'Lanciarii Augustenses' is attested among the units serving under military magistrates for Illyricum in the Notitia Dignitatum, in partibus Orientis IX.36 (ed. Seeck p.29). If that list pertains to the beginning of the fifth century, that suggests the approximate period for an attestation of such a unit. Manganaro contextualises the inscription (with text of IG) in relation to others pertaining to military figures in the C4-6 AD in eastern Sicily.
