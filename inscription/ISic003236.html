Apparatus criticus

Text from autopsy (Prag)

English translation

Phlabianos (Flavianus), worthy and blameless, lived for 37 years, 3 months, 10 days.

Italian translation

Flaviano, degno e innocente, visse 37 anni, 3 mesi, 10 giorni.

commentary

According to Paolo Orsi, the urn still contained the deceasedâ€™s ashes when it was discovered, and traces of gilding were still visible on some of the letters and the capital of one of the relief columns. The use of the name Flavianus suggests a date after the beginning of the Flavian dynasty (beginning with Vespasian, 69 AD), but Flavianusâ€™ status as slave, freedman, or free remains uncertain. The use of architectural elements in the form of the chest and a tabula ansata to frame the inscription are typical features of ash chests of this period.
