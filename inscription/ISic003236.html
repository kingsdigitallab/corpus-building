Description
A rectangular white marble urn, with a lid. The lid has acroteria on each of the four corners. The urn has square fluted columns depicted in relief on each vertical corner, and a tabula ansata in light relief fills the front face.

Lettering
Neat and regular square letters. Phi and omicron are rhomboid in form; standard four-bar sigma; epsilon has horizontal bars of equal length; alpha has a slightly extended apex; mu has extended apices, and all four strokes are full length and off-vertical. There is one instance of ligature in line 3, and interpuncts are in the form of simple triangular incisions.
