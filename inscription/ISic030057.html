Apparatus criticus

Text from Souza 2022 in ZPE.; Manganaro does not expand the phratry-tribe designations here or elsewhere in the text.
Line.4: [σὺ]ν, M. and C.
Line.13: On the lamina, the letter in the final word that must be an omega or an alpha is rendered oddly with two vertical lines.
Line.14: δυ(ω) δ[ε]κάτα, C.;
Line.15: M. read Δ̣Ε̣Υ̣; C. read [ἕκτα] δευ(τέρα); I cannot detect the existence of letters on this line either by autopsy nor photography.
Line.16: [Τ]ΡΙΚΑΙΔΕΚΑΤ++ ? ++, M.; τρί(τα) και δεκάτ(α), C. and D.

English translation

When ??? was in office...--archos son of Hagelochos sixth (phratry)-second (tribe) bought a house, a portico...in the district of Herakles, and the courtyards from the inner room...and the well and the private mill and the doorways...for five talents from Dikaiagora the daughter of Antallos. [Guarantors:] Tenth (phratry)-first (tribe). Sixth (phratry)-second (tribe). Sixth (phratry)-second (tribe). Sixth (phratry)-second (tribe). Tenth (phratry)-second (tribe). Sixth (phratry)-second (tribe) or tenth (phratry)-first (tribe). Twelfth (phratry). Sixth (phratry)-second (tribe). Thirteenth (phratry).
