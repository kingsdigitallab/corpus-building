Apparatus criticus

  • Text from autopsy;
  • 3: Bivona: XI; in theory there is room in the lacuna at the end of the line for one more character, but almost certainly not more, so XII is also possible.

Physical description

Support

Description
3 joining fragments of a white crystalline marble plaque. Finished smooth on the rear. Part of the upper left and lower right are missing. The upper right and lower left edges of the epigraphic field are preserved, but the plaque is chipped and damaged on all sides with irregular and incomplete edges. Part of a raised moulding is preserved uppper right. Some modern red paint on the sides of the plaque.
