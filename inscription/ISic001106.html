English translation

Diodoros Appeiraios, son of Tittelos, dedicates (a statue of) his sister Minyra, (wife?) of Artemon, priestess, to Aphrodite Ourania.

English translation

Diodoros Appeiraios, figlio di Tittelos, dedica (una statua della) sorella Minyra, (moglie?) di Artemon, sacerdotessa, a Afrodite Ourania.

commentary

A new text (ISic001700) found in 2021 in situ records the same Diodoros Appeiraios.
