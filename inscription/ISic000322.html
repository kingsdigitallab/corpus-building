Text after Korhonen 2004 and photograph

English translation

[...]to [...]andris, she lived 40 years; Amabilis (made this) for the best wet-nurse for being deserving; likewise to Crescens, he lived 25 years; the same Amabilis (made this) for the best husband for being deserving.
