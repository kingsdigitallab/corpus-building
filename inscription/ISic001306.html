commentary

The structure of the epitaph is the most frequent in pagan epitaphs, with the invocation to the underworld spirits, the name of the deceased in the nominative form, his/her age, the name of the dedicants (in this case, the parents) and the dedication verb in aorist (Korhonen 2004: 85-86).
