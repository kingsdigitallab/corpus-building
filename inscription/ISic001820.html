Apparatus criticus

Text of Orsi 1895

English translation

Arete passed away in Christ 8 days before the ides of July

commentary

The smaller lower left fragment was found during the 1893 excavation campaign, the main fragment in the 1895 campaign; the smaller fragment was first published in 1893, the two together in 1895, with a single inventory entry for both.
