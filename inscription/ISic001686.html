Inscription

Layout
Three lines of Latin letters, the first two with a consistent right margin, the third finishing much shorter, and a clear vacat below (up to c.50 mm) suggesting any fourth line significantly shorter. Lines 2 and 3 have guidelines along the upper edge of the line.
Text condition
incomplete
Lettering
Somewhat crudely v-cut letters of rather stocky form, with simple serifs. P is open, R closed with straight tail. G has a very curved hook. A simple dash form interpunct at end of line 2, but not between the abbreviations in line 2.
