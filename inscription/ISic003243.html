English translation

Lollis Eutyches made (this) when he was still alive for his wife Sabeina in her memory. She lived for 35 years.

Italian translation

Lollis Eutyches fece (questo) per sua moglie Sabeina mentre era ancora vivo in sua memoria. Visse 35 anni.
