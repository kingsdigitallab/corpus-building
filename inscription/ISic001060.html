Description
Block of the local limestone, originally in situ above the entrance to the rock-cut structure of the gymnasium, but then cut from the site and removed. The epigraphic field is 212 x 64 cm. The face has two large round holes cut into the second line, and a further element cut out of the end of the first line (upper right corner). The lower edge is damaged, obscuring part of line 4.
