View in current site

ἐπὶ γυμνασιάρχω̣[ν]
Ἀριστίω̣ν̣ος ((—)) [το]ῦ Ἀγαθ̣[---]
Φιλιστίωνος ((—)) τοῦ Ἐπικράτ̣[εος]
νεανίσ̣κ̣[οι Ἱερ]ώνειο[ι]

Apparatus criticus

Text checked against photograph (and rapid visual inspection); Manganaro: Ἀγαθ[άρχου]

English translation

When Aristion son of Agath[---] and Philistion son of Epikrates were gymnasiarchs, the neaniskoi Hieroneioi (dedicated this).

Italian translation

Quando Aristione figlio di Agath[---] e Filistione figlio di Epicrate erano ginnasiarchi, i neaniskoi Hieroneioi (dedicarono questo)

commentary

The text was first reported by Fazello in the 16th century, but removed from the site in 1894.
