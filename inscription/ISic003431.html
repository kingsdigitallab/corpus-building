commentary

Orsi consulted Guarducci for advice on the non-standard forms in the text. Parallels can be found in ISic001313 from Catania. The text is unusual in being carved into the brick slab after firing. The implication of the restorations conjectured for lines 2-3 is that not more than c.4 letters are missing from the personal name in line 1.
