commentary

Manganaro suggests restoring either Valentinian III (424-455 CE) or Theodosius II (402-405 CE); Ferrua argued rather for later 5th or even 6th century CE. For Busiris, see PLRE II.243-4. In part it depends upon the possible date range of the use of the formula Salvo domino nostra (attested from the Constantinian period onwards, but more common from the Theodosian period), and also the addressing of a single emperor. ISic000652 was found in close proximity.
