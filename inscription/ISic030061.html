Physical description

Support

Description
A lead sheet in eight fragments, damaged on top and right sides, apparently preserved on left and perhaps bottom sides. Reported in early publications to be silver, but apparently it is a lead object like all other Sicilian real estate contracts.

The left side of the text and its margin is completely preserved from L. 4 onward; all other margins are unknown. The text appears to wrap, e.g. from L. 4 to L. 5 where the break is between syllables.
