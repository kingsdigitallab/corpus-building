English translation

...all that goes with...upon paying it off; and he is to pay it off (sc. the debt) in a year, or in six months following it (sc. the mortgaged property) is to be Dion's. Guarantors: Aischrion son of Stratios, Stratios son of Aischrion, Philon son of Aristarchos son of Philistion. Guarantors: Aischrion son of Stratios, Aischrion son of Oltos, Philon son of Aristaios son of Philistion.
