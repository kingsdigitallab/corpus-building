Apparatus criticus

Text is from Manganaro in ASNP 1977. The main alternative is Comparetti's editio princeps in NotScav 1912.; C. adds two speculatively restored lines before the first preserved line: [πεπρᾶσθαι Δίωνι τοῦ δεῖνος τὸ τοῦ δεῖνος τοῦ δεῖνος ἐν]; [κτῆμα καὶ τ]ὰ, C.
2: [ἐπ]ὶ, C.; ἐνι[αυτῶι]( vac. ), C.
3: [ἢ ἑ]ξαμήνωι, C.
4: Ἄμ[π]οχοι, C.; Αἰσχρίων Σίρα, Ἡρ[..]στ<υ>λ[λῖ?], C.
5: νος, C.; Ἀριστάρχ[ου]( vac. ), C.
8: C. interprets the monogram as indicating a connection between the two Aischrions listed one after another; M. follows De Sanctis in considering it an error; Ἀριστά[ρχου]( vac. ), C.

English translation

...all that goes with...upon paying it off; and he is to pay it off (sc. the debt) in a year, or in six months following it (sc. the mortgaged property) is to be Dion's. Guarantors: Aischrion son of Stratios, Stratios son of Aischrion, Philon son of Aristarchos son of Philistion. Guarantors: Aischrion son of Stratios, Aischrion son of Oltos, Philon son of Aristaios son of Philistion.
