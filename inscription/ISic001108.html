Σώπολις Φαλάκ[ρου τ]ὰν αὐτοῦτα ματέρα
[---]αν Φαλ[ακρ]ίαν εὐνοίας ἕνεκα

Apparatus criticus

Text of Ampolo and Erdas 2019

English translation

Sopolis son of Phalakros (honours/dedicates) his mother [---]a Phalakria, on account of her goodwill.

Italian translation

Sopolis figlio di Phalakros (ha onorato/eretto la statua di) sua madre [---] Phalakria per la sua benvolenza[trans. Ampolo and Erdas]
