Text of IMusPalermo

English translation

To Tiberius Claudius Herodianus, a man of most illustrious fame (i.e. of senatorial class), most extraordinary judge, patron of the colony of Panhormitans. The foremost men (of the colony) gave (this monument) as a gift from communally collected coin.
