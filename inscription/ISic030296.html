English translation

In the year of Leukios son of Kaisios and Philonidas son of Phil[-], on the 4th of Adonios, resolved by the assembly as also by the council. Since under the happy guidance of fortune the affairs of the Nakonaians have been restored and it is desirable to live together in harmony in the future also, and ambassadors from the Egestaians have arrived, Apellichos son of Aleidas, Attikos son of Piston, Dionysios son of Dekios, and have given advice about what is jointly profitable to all the citizens, resolved to bring together on the 4th of Adonios the citizens and to invite those of the citizens among whom dissension has arisen while they have been competing for control of public affairs to come to the assembly and to reach a settlement among themselves, thirty individuals from each group having been listed in advance; those who have been opponents in previous times are to make the list each choosing from the other side. The archons, drawing the names by lot, having placed them separately into two hydriai, are to select by lot one of each group and from the rest of the citizens select another three in addition to the two, excluding those relationships which the law requires to be excluded from juries. Those who have been drawn by lot into the same group are to be chosen brothers keeping in harmony with one another with all justice and friendship. And when all the sixty lots are taken up and those allotted in addition to them, they are to allot the remainder of the citizens to groups of five, excluding the family relationships already specified, and these too, in the same way as the previously allotted groups, are to be brothers in their allotted groups. The hieromnamones are to offer in sacrifice a white she-goat and the treasurer is to provide what is required for the sacrifice. Simliarly all succeeding magistrates are to sacrifice to the Ancestors and to Harmony each year on this day a victim for each group, whatever they may decide, and the citizens are to feast together in their fraternal groups. The archons are to inscribe this decree on a bronze plaque and exhibit it in the pronaos of Zeus Olympios.
