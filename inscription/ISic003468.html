English translation

To Marcus Vallius Rufus son of Gaius, of tribe Quirina, honoured with the public horse by the deified Augustus Antoninus Pius, the plebs of Gaulus [set this up] from community collected money on account of his merits and to honour his father Gaius Vallius Postumus, the patron of the municipum [---]
