ISic003022. I.Sicily inscription 003022

Facsimile Images

σωφ[ρο]σύνην τιμῶσα
δικαιοσύνην τε σέβου[σ]α
[Ἱ]ππὼ ἐν ἡλικίαι πνεῦμ’ ἔ[λ]ι̣πεν βιότου̣

Apparatus criticus

Text after Guarducci 1987 with slight changes based on photograph

English translation

Honouring wisdom and venerating justice, in his youth Hippò left the breath of life.

Italian translation

Guarducci: Onorando la saggezza e venerando la giustizia, Hippò in gioventù il soffio lasciò della vita.
