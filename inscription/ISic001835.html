commentary

It is not possible to say whether the final letter is an Iota or another letter beginning with a vertical, given the damage to the stone. It is possible that this bore same ΔΒ as on ISic001834; but Β, Γ, Ε, Η, Ι, Κ, Μ, Ν, Π, Ρ are all theoretically possible.
