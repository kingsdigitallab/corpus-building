commentary

This document is one of a large deposit (c. 150) of lead tesserae found in a storeroom of Athena's sanctuary at the archaeological site of Kamarina. The tessera seems to have been used twice (see Cordano 1992, p. 45). The first use can be associated with Σάκις on face a and on face b Πολυκλέος, the patronym referring to the name Σάκις; the second use seems to be associated with line 2, [---]ο̄ν Καλαίκο̄ on face a, and [---] Καλαίκο̄ on face b, written in smaller characters.
