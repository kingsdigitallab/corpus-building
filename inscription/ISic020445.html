Apparatus criticus

Text after Arena 1989 (IGASM I) with slight changes based on photograph. ; Guarducci: hαύτα δὲ μά (= μή)

English translation

I am Aristocleias' the beautiful the beautiful (lekythos), and she is mine; Pithacos after demanding her owns her.

Italian translation

Io sono la bella (lekythos) della bella Aristocleias, e lei è mia; Pithacos dopo averla reclamata la possiede.
