Apparatus criticus

Text of Cordano 2005

translation

Asklepiades, farewell!

commentary

The text implies the vocative + salutation formula which is found several times at Mineo, and reproduced in the latin epitaph engraved on the reverse of this stone (ISic003447). The text is said to come from Monte Catalfaro, c.3km NE of Mineo.
