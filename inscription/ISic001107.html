Text of Ampolo and Erdas 2019

English translation

The people of the Segestans (honours/dedicates) Phalakros Eyrssios, son of Diodoros, on account of his virtue.

Italian translation

Il popolo dei Segestani (ha onorato/ha eretto la statua di) Phalakros, figlio di Diodoros, Eyrssios, per il suo valore [trans. Ampolo and Erdas]
