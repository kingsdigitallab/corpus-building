Apparatus criticus

Text of Bitto 2001

English translation

Cottia Euphrosyne set this (statue) up out of her own money for Cerrinia Cottia, daughter of Lucius, a very dutiful daughter, in the place determined by the decuriones. She distributed denarii in return for the dedication of these (statues).
