Description
The monument on which the inscription is placed is a slab of local limestone decorated with bas-relief and figures in the round. The representation on the front face is obtained by the superimposition of a circular shield, a groom's left leg seen in profile and a horse moving towards the left. The monument is carved on three sides, measuring 125 cm wide, 70 cm high and 70 cm deep.
