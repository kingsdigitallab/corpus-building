ISic004269. Inscription naming Tiberius draft

View in current site

( vac. ) Ti(berio) Caesari divi [Augusti f(ilio) --- Augusto]

Apparatus criticus

Text according to ILipara 753

English translation

Tiberius Caesar [Augustus], son of the divine [Augustus ---].

Italian translation

Tiberius Caesar [Augustus], figlio del divo [Augustus ---].

commentary

Manganaro suggested that this was part of a single text for Tiberius, combined with ISic004268; Orsi instead considered it a separate text, this for Tiberius, the other for Augustus, but probably belonging to the same monument, and the editors of I.Lipara followed Orsi. Certainly the two texts in Orsi's drawing show considerable similarities and the fact of their re-use together likewise suggests that they belong together, but whether as one or two texts cannot be resolved.
