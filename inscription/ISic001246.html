Inscription

Layout
The text is inscribed on three faces, the precise organisation of which remains a question of discussion. Battistoni 2020 and 2011, after Bormann, offers the most plausible and complete reconstruction, followed here: Section A in two columns on face I (left face for the viewer), continuing upper left on face III (central face for the viewer); Section B in a single column on face II (right face for the viewer); Section C, top of face III, followed by a large lacuna; Section D, lower left of face III; Section E.i, traces top right of face III, followed by a large lacuna; section E.ii, lower right of face III.
