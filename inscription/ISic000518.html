ex aere conlato patrono
bene de se merito curante
et de suo quod defuerat
supplente
5Paconio Clodiano

Apparatus criticus

Text from Gualtherus; Mommsen adds interpuncts throughout, but these are not recorded by Gualtherus and so have no basis in fact.

translation

commentary

Mommsen notes the smiilarity to CIL 10.7294 = ISic000551 and so speculates that this might be a doublet in the antiquarian tradition.
