commentary

The text appears to be on a relief, possibly funerary, of which Gualtherus provides a drawing. There is no record of autopsy after Gualtherus and it appears to be lost. Kaibel's attempt to suggest a comprehensible text ignores the presentation of Gualtherus; Kaibel's reference to Gualtherus fails to indicate that the text is in the addenda, p.100, not the main sequence.
