ISic001330. Epitaph draft

View in current site

[---]
ἔζη(σεν) ((·)) ( vac. 1) ἔτη λ̣[.]´
Ὑγία ἐποίσε
μνήμης χάριν

Apparatus criticus

Text based on photographs; Ferrara: κ; Kaibel: δ, Korhonen: λ (less probable α because of ἔτη)

English translation

(name) lived 3- years. Hygia built (this) for his/her memory.

Italian translation

(nome) visse 3- anni. Hygia realizzò (questo) a sua memoria.

commentary

The epitaph presents the name of the deceased (in the lacuna) and the indication of age. A second sentence contains the name of the dedicant, the dedication verb (in the form ἐποίσε, attested in other two inscriptions of Catania, IMusCatania 65 = ISic003223 and IG 14.1852 = ISic003246, see Korhonen 2004: 195-196) and the formula μνήμης χάριν. The name Ὑγία/Ὑγίεια is frequently attested in the imperial age also in Sicily (LGPN 3A: 439).
