Apparatus criticus

  • Text after Lo Faro in Marchese 2012 ;
  • 1-3: ἤκε and Βῶκυς are written smaller and adjoins the ends of ll. 1-3. Lo Faro (2012) agrees with Agnello (1950-1951) that the smaller text is an addition to the main text and is meant to specify the name of the deceased (vel quidem Bochus or Bucco).
