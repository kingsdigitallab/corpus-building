Apparatus criticus

Text of IGPalermo but corrected against photograph; note that Kaibel followed Alessi's apograph, which does not observe the original layout.; Alessi: ΠΡΟΤΑ; Kirchoff: προτ[έρ]ᾳ; Manni Piraino: πρὸ ⟨ι⟩α’; the two numerals ι α are clearly distinguished by interpuncts and a line above, which joins the top of the iota (whence misinterpretation as Τ).
