commentary

This document is one of a large deposit (c. 150) of lead tesserae found in a storeroom of Athena's sanctuary at the archaeological site of Kamarina. The lamina appears to be used twice (see Cordano 1992, pp. 58-59). In the second use, face a is used as face b and vice versa. The first engraving, the one related to the first use, uses smaller letters than the second one. The first text has not been erased in order to engrave the second, therefore it is clearly visible. For a similar reuse of tesserae see also ISic030197 and ISic030198.
