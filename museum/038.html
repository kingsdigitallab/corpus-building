Museo Civico Gaetano Savasta

A small series of display cases containing material from Neolithic to recent times, from excavations in the local area: San Marco, Poggio Monaco, Monte Castellaccio, Poggio Cocola and the Collina Storica. Principally ceramics, with some small finds
