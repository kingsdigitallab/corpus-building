Museo Civico di Gangi

The archaeological collection is arranged in 4 rooms on the ground floor, and is mainly devoted to 6th and 5th century BC material from the surrounding area. There is some Roman material (principally oil lamps and the 3 inscriptions). One room is entirely focused on the site of Monte Alburchia (from which the 3 inscriptions originate)

Corso Giuseppe Fedele Vitale 54, 90024 - Gangi (PA)
