Museo della cultura e delle Arti Figurative Bizantine e Normanne

A beautiful collection in a converted church, mainly consisting of stray finds from around town. An excellent epigraphic collection and some material from wells excavated when the church was converted. A highlight is a so-called Kotabos stand

Location

Via Ferraloro 96, 98070 - San Marco D'alunzio (ME)

San Marco d'Alunzio, Messina, Italy
