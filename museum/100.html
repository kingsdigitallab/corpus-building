Museo Civico di Noto museum

The museum was damaged in an earthquake and all the ancient archaeological material was sent to the Paolo Orsi for safe keeping. They have not yet been returned and the collection currently consists of stones from various medieval buildings, some medieval armour and weaponry, and 2 ancient inscribed blocks
