Museo Civico Pippo Rizzo museum

Arranged across 2 floors and a series of rooms, the upper floor follows a chronological arrangement beginning with the Neolithic and Bronze Age, the 5-4 century BC site of Montagna Vecchia, then some Hellenistic and Roman finds (including the famous milestone). The lower floor is a lapidarium with some reconstructed pithoi. Several columns and architrave pieces come from 2 Roman peristyle houses
