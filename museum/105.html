Museo Regionale Agostino Pepoli museum

The archaeological collection is housed in one long room that has been subdivided into 4 discrete areas containing 25 cases and a series of open displays. 1 case contains pre- and proto-historic material, 3 are for material from Selinunte, 1 Camarina, 8 Erice, 1 Mozia, 1 Birgi, 1 Lilybaeum, 1 Centuripe, 4 contain material of unknown origin, 1 mixed sites, and then 1 for Punic stamped amphora handles, 1 for Greek and Roman stamped amphora handles, and 1 for Latin epigraphy. In addition there are open displays of material found on the seabed (amphorae, anchor parts, a Roman ram from the Aegadi Islands battle), a large two-sided Latin inscription and a section on Arabic and later Jewish inscriptions
