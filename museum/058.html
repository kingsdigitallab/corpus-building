Museo Civico Polivalente museum

The museum moved from Via Monte 1 in 2010, and is now housed in a recently restored palace built by the Tasca D'Almerita family. The archaeological collection is displayed in random order, with few labels or descriptions. Late material is mixed with early, and although there are a few cases with fragmentary ceramics of various periods and two coins, the focus is on various stones (some architectural, some not).
