Polimuseo Attilio Zarino museum

A small but relatively rich collection of material from the area around Vittoria. Some are labelled as being from Camarina, some from closer to the museum. There is an inscription, some pottery, some coins and a few other miscellaneous objects. The collection has been declared and gifted to the state, and it is hoped that it will soon be moved to a building designed specifically for it
