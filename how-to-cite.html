How to Cite

To cite this I.Sicily website containing the corpus and edition of inscriptions with aggregated annotations (palaeographic, petrographic and linguistic), please use the following:

Prag, Jonathan R.W. (ed.), with Alex Antoniou, Alessia Coccato, Robert Crellin, Victoria Fendel, Marta Fogagnolo, Ilenia Gradante, Michael Metcalfe, Valentina Mignosa, Francesca Prado, Flavio Santini, Simona Stoyanova, Alfredo Tosques, King’s Digital Lab (tech ed.), with Arianna Ciula, Neil Jakeman, Ryan Heuser, Zihao Lu, Geoffroy Noël, Tiffany Ong, Miguel Vieira. 2017-2025. I.Sicily: Inscriptions of Ancient Sicily. sicily.classics.ox.ac.uk. Stable deposit: DOI ISicily/ISicily:work-in-progress (12-04-2024).

See credits and acknowledgments for details on specific contributions.
